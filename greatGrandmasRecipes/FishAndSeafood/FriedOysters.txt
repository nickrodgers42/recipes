# Fried Oysters
a:
* 1 pint fresh oysters
* 1 egg slightly beaten with 1 T water
* 1/2 C flour
* 1/2 t paprika
* 1 C sifted bread crumbs
* 1/2 t salt

> Dip oysters in flour which has been mixed with salt and paprika. Then dip in egg, then in crumbs. Fry in hot oil (375 degrees) until golden brown. Drain on absorbent paper.
> Serves 6.
> Fred liked oysters cooked any way.
