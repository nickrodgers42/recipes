# Chicken-crab Casserole
a:
* 1/2 C butter
* 3/4 t salt
* 2 T finely chopped onion
* 3/4 t paprika
* 7 T flour
* 1/2 t rosemary, crushed
* 1 7 oz can flaked crabmeat
* 2 C chicken broth
* 2 C dairy sour cream
* 3 C cooked chicken pieces

> Cook onion in butter until golden. Blend in flour and seasonings and cook until bubbly.
> Remove from heat and stir in chicken broth. Return to heat and bring to a boil, stirring constantly, for 1 to 2 minutes. Remove from heat and blend in sour cream little by little.
> Add chicken and crab meat. Turn into a greased, 2 quart baking dish. Top with bread crumbs that have been mixed with 2 T melted butter. Bake at 350 degrees 25 to 30 minutes or until topping is golden brown. Serves 10 to 12.
> This won first place in the Queen of the Cooks contest at the Dairy Festival.
