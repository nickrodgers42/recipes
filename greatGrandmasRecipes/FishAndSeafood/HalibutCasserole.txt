# Halibut Casserole
a:
* 2 C cubed halibut or turbot
* 1 C chopped celery
* 2 hard boiled eggs, chopped
* 3/4 C shredded sharp cheese
* 3 T butter
* 1 1/2 C milk
* 1/2 C chopped green pepper
* 1/8 t paprika
* 1 C cooked noodles
* 1/2 C mayonnaise
* 3 T flour
* 1 t salt

> Saute green pepper and celery in butter until soft. Add halibut (about 1 lb, may still be frozen), chopped eggs and cooked noodles. Melt butter, add flour and blend in milk, stirring while cooking until smooth. Stir in salt, pepper, paprika, mayonnaise, and cheese. Blend well. Add sauce to halibut mixture. Place in buttered casserole and cover with buttered crumbs. Bake for 45 minutes at 350 degrees.
