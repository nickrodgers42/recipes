# Maryland Crab Shells
a:
@

* 40 Jumbo pasta shells(1 lb)
* 2 T green pepper, chopped
* 1 T red onion, chopped
* 1/4 C (A stick) butter
* 1 1/2 lbs imitation crab meat
* 1/2 t black pepper
* 1 t Old Bay Seasoning
* 1 egg, slightly beaten
* 2 1/2 C milk
* 1 C mayonnaise
* 3 T all-purpose flour
* 2 oz grated parmesan cheese

> Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees. Cook shells according to directions on package. Rinse and cool. Saute green pepper and onion in 1 T butter until softened, 3 to 5 minutes.
> Combine crabmeat, sauteed vegetables, black pepper, 1/2 t Old Bay Seasoning, egg, 1/2 C milk and mayonnaise in a large bowl. Melt A T butter in each of two 11 x 7 x 2 inch baking dishes. Spoon filling into shells. Arrange filled shells in each baking dish. Melt remaining 2 T butter in a saucepan over medium-Iow heat. Whisk in flour; gradually whisk in remaining milk; cook until slightly thickened. Stir in parmesan cheese and drizzIe sauce evenly over sheIIs. Serves 8. (659 calories, 35 grams fat, 53 grams carbohydrate, 687 milligrams sodium.)
