# Oysters In Batter
a:Fredâ€™s favorite
* 1 quart oysters
* 2 eggs
* 2/3 C milk
* 1 C sifted flour
* 1 t salt pepper

> Drain oysters thoroughly and blot between paper towels. Beat eggs, stir in milk and add to flour and seasonings. Beat until smooth. Dip oysters in batter and drop in deep fat heated to 365 degrees. Fry 3 to 5 minutes to desired brownness. Drain on absorbent paper. Serve with tartar or chili sauce. This batter is very good for any fish deep fried, or pan fried. Serves 5.
