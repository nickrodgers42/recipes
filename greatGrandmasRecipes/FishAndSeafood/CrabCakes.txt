# Crab Cakes
a:
@

* 8 oz fresh or imitation crab meat
* salt and pepper to taste
* 1 egg
* 1 to 2 cloves fresh garlic
* 1 T mayonnaise
* 2 t Dijon mustard
* 1 T fresh cilantro, finely chopped
* 4 green onions finely sliced, include green part
* 2 to 3 oz dried bread crumbs(set aside)

> Put all ingredients in a bowl and mix until blended. Add enough breadcrumbs to bind.
> Quarter the mixture and pat each one into a flat. cake about 3/4 inch thick. Fry the cakes in a little oil until browned on both sides. Serve with lemon wedges and a green or tomato salad.
