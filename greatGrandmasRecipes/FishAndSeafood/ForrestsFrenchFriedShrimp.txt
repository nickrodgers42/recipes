# Forrest's French Fried Shrimp
a:The Bubba-Gump Shrimp Company
@

* 2 lbs fresh shrimp
* 1 C all purpose flour
* 1 1/2 t baking powder
* 1/2 t salt
* 2/3 C water
* 3 T lemon juice
* 1 T vegetable oil
* 1 large egg, lightly beaten

> Peel and de-vein shrimp, leaving tails intact. Combine flour, baking powder, and salt in a medium bowl. Add water, lemon juice and vegetable oil. Stir until smooth. Dip shrimp into batter, let excess drip off and fry, a few at a time, until golden. Drain on paper towels.
