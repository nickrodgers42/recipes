# Crab Casserole
a:Elizabeth Thorpe
* 1 can crab meat
* 6 boiled eggs, diced
* 1 C fresh bread crumbs
* 1 C mayonnaise
* 1/2 t dried parsley (1 T minced parsley)
* 2 t minced onion
* 1 pint canned milk or half and half
* 1/2 C buttered bread crumbs for top

> Combine ingredients and put into buttered 1/2 quart casserole. Top with buttered crumbs. Bake 20 to 30 minutes at 375 degrees. Serves 8.
