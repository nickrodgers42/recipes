# Clam Chowder
a:
* 6 medium potatoes, peeled and cubed
* 2 T dried onions (1/2 C diced fresh)
* 4 strips bacon, crisply fried, crumbled
* 1 carrot, grated
* 1 can minced clams
* 1 C evaporated milk
* pepper
* salt

> Cook potatoes and carrots in water to cover until tender. Remove about 1 C of potatoes and blend till smooth; add again to soup. After frying bacon, drain off some of the fat and then oook onion in same pan, until transparent. Add clams and evaporated milk and heat, then add to soup. Heatjust to boiling. Garnish with bacon bits. Serve hot.
