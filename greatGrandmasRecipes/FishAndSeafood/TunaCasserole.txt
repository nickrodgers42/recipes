# Tuna Casserole
a:Loye Roberts
* 1 pkg Lipton Noodle Soup Mix (crumble seasonings)
* 1/2 can mushroom soup
* 1/2 C un-cooked rice
* 1 T chopped onion
* 2 1/2 C milk
* 1 can tuna
* 1 T chopped pimento (optional)
* crushed potato chips for toping

> Mix and put in buttered baking dish. Bake 1 hour at 325 degrees.
