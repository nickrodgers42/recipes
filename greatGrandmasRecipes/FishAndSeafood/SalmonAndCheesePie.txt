# Salmon And Cheese Pie
a:
* 2 C biscuit mix
* 1 egg, separated
* 6 T milk can
* 1 can (7 3/4 oz) salmon
* 1/4 C chopped green onions
* 1/4 C chopped green pepper
* 2 T butter
* 1 T flour
* 1/2 t celery salt
* 1 whole egg
* 1 C large curd cottage cheese
* 2 T minced parsley
* 2 T grated Parmesan cheese

> Combine biscuit mix, egg yolk and milk and mix with fork until it forms a soft dough. Roll out on a lightly floured surface to an 11 inch circle. Fit into a 9 inch pie pan; trim and flute edge reserving trimmings. Drain and flake salmon, reserving liquid. Saute onions and pepper in butter until tender. Blend in flour and celery salt. Gradually add salmon liquid, cooking and stirring until thickened. Stir in flaked salmon. With electric mixer, beat whole egg, egg white, cottage cheese, Parmesan cheese and parsley until blended.
> Spoon over salmon mixture. Cut 6 diamond shapes or make strips from pastry trimmiings. Arrange on top of pie. Bake at 375 degrees 25 to 30 minutes, or until top is set. Serves 6.
