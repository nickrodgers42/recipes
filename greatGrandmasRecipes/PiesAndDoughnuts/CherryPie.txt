# Cherry Pie
a:
* 2 1/2 t Minute Tapioca
* 1 C sugar
* 3 C drained sour cherries
* 1/8 t salt
* 6 drops red coloring
* 1/2 C cherry juice
* 1/4 t almond extract

> Mix together and let stand 15 minutes. Pour into 9 inch pie shell; dot with 1 t butter. Add top crust. Bake at 425 degrees for about 50 minutes.
