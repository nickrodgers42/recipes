# Apple Pie
a:
* 2 1/2 to 3 C peeled, sliced, tart apples
* 3/4 C sugar
* 1/4 t cinnamon
* 2 to 4 T butter
* 1/2 t salt

> Mix dry ingredients; pour over apples in pastry lined pan. Dot generously with butter.
> Wet edges of under crust; Cover with upper crust which has small openings cut in it, or make slashes after covering pie. Press edges together and flute edges if desired. Sprinkle a little sugar on top crust. Bake at 425 degrees for 35 to 40 minutes.
