# Rhubarb Pie
a:
* 2 C rhubarb
* 1 C sugar
* 2 T flour
* 1/4 t salt
* 1 beaten egg butter

> Wash and dry rhubarb; cut into 1/2 inch pieces. Mix with other ingredients. Pour into 8 inch pastry lined pie pan. Dot generously with butter. Put on top crust, sealing moistened edges, and making a few small openings for steam to escape. Sprinkle with sugar. Bake 30 to 35 minutes at 425 degrees.
