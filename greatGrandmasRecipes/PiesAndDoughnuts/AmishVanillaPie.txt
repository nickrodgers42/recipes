# Amish Vanilla Pie
a:
* 1/2 C brown sugar, packed
* 1 T flour
* 1/4 C dark corn syrup
* 1 1/2 t vanilla
* 1 egg, beaten
* 1 C water
* 1 C un-sifted flour
* 1/2 C brown sugar, packed
* 2 t cream of tartar
* 1/2 t baking soda
* 1/8 t salt
* 1/4 C butter

> Combine 1/2 C brown sugar,1 T flour, syrup, vanilla and egg in 2 quart saucepan. Slowly stir in water. Cook over medium heat, stirring, until mixture comes to a boil. Cool.
> Combine 1 C flour, 1/2 C brown sugar, cream of tartar, soda. salt and butter; Mix until crumbly. Pour cooled mixture into 9 inch pie shell; top with crumbs. Bake at 350 degrees for 40 minutes or until golden brown.
