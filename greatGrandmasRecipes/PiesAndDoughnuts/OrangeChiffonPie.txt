# Orange Chiffon Pie
a:
* 2 C chocolate wafer crumbs
* 3 egg yolks
* 1/4 C melted butter
* 3/4 C sugar
* 1 envelope gelatin, unflavored
* 1 6 oz can frozen orange juice, thawed
* 1 C water
* 1/2 C semi-sweet chocolate pieces, melted
* 2 T lemon juice
* 3 egg whites, stiffly beaten
* 1 C heavy cream, whipped

> Mix crumbs with butter. Press evenly over bottom and sides of a 9 inch pie pan. Chill.
> Soak gelatin in 1/2 C water for 5 minutes. Combine egg yolks, sugar, orange juice, lemon juice and remaining 1/2 C water. Cook over low heat stirring constantly, until mixture coats a spoon. Do not boii. Add softened gelatin, and stir until gelatin is dissolved. Chili until slightly thickened. Fold in beaten egg whites and whipped cream. Add half the melted chocolate. and stir only until chocolate is swirled evenly through mixture. Pour into crumb-Iined pie pan. Using a large spoon, drizzle remaining melted chocolate in fine lines from center of pie to outside edges. Chiil until firm.
