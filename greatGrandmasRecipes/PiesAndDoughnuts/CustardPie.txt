# Custard Pie
a:

> Beat 4 eggs until light. and slowly add 1/2 C sugar. Add 2 C warm milk, 1/2 t vanilla. 1/2 t lemon, and 1/4 t salt. Cover an 8 or 9 inch pie pan with rich pastry. Brush egg white over bottom or spread soft butter over pastry. Add custard filling and bake slowly at 325 degrees for 40 to 50 minutes. (This is one of the favorite pies I remember my mother making).
