# Cream Puffs
a:
* 1 1/4 C water
* 1/2 C butter
* 1 1/4 C sifted flour
* 1/2 t salt
* 4 eggs
* 1 can sweetened condensed  (CHOCOLATE SAUCE)
* Milk 1/2 to 1 C water (CHOCOLATE SAUCE)
* 2 squares un-sweetened chocolate  (CHOCOLATE SAUCE)
* 1 t vanilla (CHOCOLATE SAUCE)
* 1/8 t sat (CHOCOLATE SAUCE)
* 1/2 C sugar (LEMON SAUCE)
* 1 T cornstarch (LEMON SAUCE)
* 1 C boiling water  (LEMON SAUCE)
* 2 T butter (LEMON SAUCE)
* 1 T grated lemon rind  (LEMON SAUCE)
* 5 T lemon juice or to taste  (LEMON SAUCE)
* 1/8 t salt. (LEMON SAUCE)
* 2 C water (CARAMEL SAUCE)
* 1 C brown sugar (CARAMEL SAUCE)
* pinch salt (CARAMEL SAUCE)
* 2 T cornstarch (CARAMEL SAUCE)
* 2 squares un-sweetened chocolate (QUICK CHOCOLATE SAUCE)
* 1 C light corn syrup (QUICK CHOCOLATE SAUCE)
* 1/2 t vanilla (QUICK CHOCOLATE SAUCE)
* 1 T butter (QUICK CHOCOLATE SAUCE)

> Sift flour and salt. Combine butter and water in saucepan and bring to a boil. Add flour mixture all at once and stir vigorously for 2 to 3 minutes, cooking over low heat until mixture forms a ball and leaves the sides of the pan. Remove from heat and cool slightly. Add eggs one at a time beating thoroughly after each addition. Continue beating until thick dough is formed. Drop by spoons—full on greased baking sheet. about 2 inches apart. Bake in a 425 degree oven for 15 minutes. Lower heat to 325 degrees and bake 25 to 30 minutes longer, until shells are thoroughly dry. Makes 24 small puffs. When cool, slit and fill with desired filling. Choose a sauce to put over top just before serving.
> CUSTARD FlLLING: Add 1 C sweetened whipped cream to any custard type pudding or pie filling, and add to puffs just before sewing. If puffs are iced, use same flavor as filling.
> CHOCOLATE SAUCE: In top of double boiler put milk, chocolate and salt. Cook over boiling water, stirring often until thickened. Remove from heat. Slowly stir in hot water until sauce is of desirable thickness. Stir in vanilla extract. Serve hot or cold.
> LEMON SAUCE: Combine Sugar and cornstarch. Dissolve in boiling water. Cook slowly, stirring constantly untiléhickened and clear. Remove from heat and add lemon juice and rind. Stir. Makes 1 1/2 C.
> CARMEL SAUCE: Mix water. sugar, and salt. Bring to boil and thicken with 2 T oomstarch. Boil 2 to 3 minutes. Remove from heat and add 1/2 t vanilla and A square of butter. For richer sauce, cool and add 1 C sweetened, whipped cream.
> QUICK CHOCOLATE SAUCE: Melt chocolate in syrup over low heat. Add vanilla and butter. Makes 1 cup.
