# Lemon Jell-o Pie
a:
* 1 3 oz pkg lemon Jell-o
* 1 C hot water
* 1/4 C lemon juice
* 3/4 C sugar

> Mix Jell-o and put aside until it starts to set. Whip Jell-o and add 1 C canned milk which has been chilled and whipped. Pour into a chilled graham cracker crust and sprinkle a layer of crumbs on top of the pie. Chill. (This was Trudy Fehlman's prize winner at a 6th ward food fair).
