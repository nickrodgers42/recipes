# My First Pie Crust
a:
* 14 T lard and butter mixed
* 2 C flour
* 1 t salt
* 6 to 8 T ice water

> Sift flour and salt. Cut in lard with fork or pastry blender, until size of small peas, if flaky pastry is desired; cut finer for mealy pastry. Add water gradually, using just enough to hold ingredients together. Work lightly; Do not handle more than necessary. Roll out, prick and bake at 425 degrees for 10 to 15 minutes. Makes 2 8 inch crusts.
