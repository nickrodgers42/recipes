# Strawberry Chiffon Pie With Cornflake Pastry
a:
* 1 T gelatin
* 3 eggs, separated
* 1 1/2 C sugar
* 1/2 t salt
* 1/2 C water
* 1 T lemon juice
* 1 pint strawberries, crushed

> Soak gelatin in half the water and set aside. Combine the egg yolks. lemon juice, the rest of the water, half the sugar and salt in a double boiler and cook until of custard consistency. Stir in soaked gelatin and remove from heat. Cool; add strawberries and egg whites beaten with the remaining sugar. Spread mixture into oomflake crust. Chill.
> Top with whipped cream.
