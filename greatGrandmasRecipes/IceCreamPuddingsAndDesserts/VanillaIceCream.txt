# Vanilla Ice Cream
a:

> Scald 4 T flour with 1 C sugar. Mix well and add 1 t salt, 4 T flour, 2 C sugar. Cook 1/2 hour in double boiler, stirring occasionally. Pour into 4 beaten egg yolks and cook 5 minutes longer. Cool and add 6 C milk, 4 egg whites beaten, 1 T vanilla, 1 T lemon extract, 3 C whipping cream, and 12 oz can evaporated milk. Freeze in mechanical freezer. Makes 6 quarts.
