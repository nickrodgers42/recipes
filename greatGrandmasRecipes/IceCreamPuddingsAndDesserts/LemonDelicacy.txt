# Lemon Delicacy
a:
* 2 T butter
* 2 eggs, separated
* juice of 1 lemon
* 1/2 lemon rind, grated
* 3/4 C sugar
* 3 T flour
* 1 C milk

> Cream together butter and sugar and well beaten egg yolks. Add flour, lemon juice and rind. Add milk, and gently fold in stiffly beaten egg whites. Pour mixture into buttered baking dish and set in a pan of hot water. Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes. Serves 4.
