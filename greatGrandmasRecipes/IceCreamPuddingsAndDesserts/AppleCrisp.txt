# Apple Crisp
a:

> Put 6 C peeled apple slices into a buttered baking dish. Sprinkle with cinnamon and 1/4 C water. In a bowl combine 1 C brown sugar, 3/4 C sifted flour and 1/3 C butter. Cut butter into mixed dry ingredients until it looks like coarse meal. Sprinkle over apples. Bake at 350 degrees for 40 to 50 minutes. Especially good served warm with homemade ice cream. Serves 6.
