# Peppermint Stick Ice Cream
a:
* 3/4 C sugar
* 1/2 C light corn syrup
* 1 t vanilla
* 1/8 t salt
* 2 C heavy cream
* 1 C light cream
* 1/2 C crushed peppermint stick candy

> Mix all together; stir until sugar dissolves. Freeze in 2 quart electric or hand crank freezer following manufacturers directions. Makes 2 quarts.
