# Cherry Torte
a:
* 1 1/2 C sugar
* 1 t melted butter
* 2 C drained pie cherries
* 1/2 C chopped nuts
* 1 C sifted flour
* 1/4 t salt
* 1 egg, well beaten
* 1 t cinnamon
* 1 t soda

> Combine sugar, flour, soda, cinnamon, salt and nuts. In another bowl mix together egg, cherries and butter. Combine the 2 mixes and stir until smooth and moist. Bake in buttered 8 inch square pan at 350 degrees for 35 to 40 minutes. Garnish with whipped cream sweetened. Makes 8 servings.
