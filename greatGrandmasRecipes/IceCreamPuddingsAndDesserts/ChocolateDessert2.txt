# Chocolate Dessert
a:
* 1/2 C butter
* 1 C powdered sugar
* 3 eggs, separated
* 2 oz unsweetened chocolate
* 1 C crushed vanilla wafers
* 1 C salad marshmallows
* 1 C nuts, broken
* 1 C cream, whipped and sweetened

> Cream butter and sugar until fluffy. Add egg yolks and beat. Gradually beat in melted chocolate. Add nuts. Fold in beaten egg whites. Beat again. Put 1/2 C crushed wafers in 9 x 9 inch pan. Carefully spoon mixture over crumbs. Set in refrigerator. Combine whipped cream with remaining ingredients. Spread over top of chocolate mixture. Top with remaining crumbs. Refrigerate several hours. Serves 9.
