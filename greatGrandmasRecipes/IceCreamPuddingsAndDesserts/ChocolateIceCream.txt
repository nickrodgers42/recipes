# Chocolate Ice Cream
a:Cherrill Nielsen
* 3 to 4 squares bitter chocolate, melted
* 5 C milk
* 2 1/2 C sugar
* 5 T flour
* 4 eggs, lightly beaten
* 5 C cream
* 3/4 t salt
* 2 t vanilla

> Scald milk over low heat. Add chocolate. Combine dry ingredients and add to scalded milk. Cook until thickened. Pour into beaten eggs and cook a few minutes more, stirring constantly. Cool and add cream and vanilla. Freeze.
