# Luetta's Pudding
a:Luetta Baugh
* 1 1/2 C butter
* 3 C sugar
* 3 C carrots, grated
* 3 C apples, grated
* 4 1/2 C flour
* 2 T soda
* 3 1/2 T baking powder
* 1 1/2 t cinnamon
* 1 1/2 t nutmeg
* 1 1/2 t allspice
* 1 T cocoa
* 1 1/2 C seedless raisins or dates
* 1 1/2 C seeded raisins
* 1 1/2 C nuts
* 1 1/2 C milk
* 1 T vanilla
* 6 eggs
* 1 pkg fruit cake mix

> Cream butter and sugar. Sift all dry ingredients together 5 times. Beat eggs and mix altogether. Fill #2 1/2 cans about 2/3 full. Seal once; steam cook 75 minutes. Seal 2nd time; Pressure cook 1 hour and 10 minutes at 10 lbs.
