# Apple Flip
a:

> 6 medium apples, peeled and chopped fine; 1 C sugar dissolved in 2 C water. Let boil while mixing the dough: Sift together 2 C flour, 2 T baking powder and 1/2 C sugar. Cut in 2 T butter. Mix with milk to make soft dough. Roll out on floured board to A inch thick.
> Spread with apples, sprinkle with cinnamon, dot with butter and roll up like a cinnamon roll. Cut in 1 inch pieces; place in hot syrup in flat baking pan on end, 1/2 inch a part.
> Bake at 350 degrees for about 40 minutes or until nicely browned. Serve hot or warm with whipped or plain cream and sugar.
