# Sweet Potato Surprise
a:
* 4 C yams or sweet potatos, mashed
* 3 eggs
* 2 t cinnamon
* 1 t salt
* 1 can evaporated milk
* 1 t ginger
* 1 C brown sugar
* 1/4 C butter
* 1 butter recipe yellow cake mix
* 3/4 C butter
* 1 C chopped nuts

> Mix first 8 ingredients and pour in 9 x 13 inch pan. Sprinkle cake mix on top. Melt 3/4 C butter and drizzle over cake mix. Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes.
