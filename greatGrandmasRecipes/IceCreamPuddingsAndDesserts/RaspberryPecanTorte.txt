# Raspberry Pecan Torte
a:
* 1 yellow cake mix
* 1 C chopped pecans
* 2 C whipped cream, sweetened
* 12 oz frozen raspberries, partially defrosted

> Make mix according to directions using 4 egg yolks in place of egg, save the whites.
> Spoon batter into 2 greased and floured 9- inch cake pans. Beat egg whites stiff with 3/4 C sugar. Divide and spread over cake batter in two pans. Sprinkle meringue with chopped pecans. Bake in 350 degree oven 30 to 35 minutes or until done. Cool 10 minutes, then turn out and immediately turn right side up. Cool thoroughly. To serve, spread one layer with half the whipped cream and half the partially thawed raspberries.
> Top with 2nd layer, then spread with remaining raspberries and whipped cream. Garnish with pecan halves. Makes 12 to 16 servings.
