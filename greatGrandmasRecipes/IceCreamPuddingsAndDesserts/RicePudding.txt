# Rice Pudding
a:
* 1/2 C rice
* 5 C milk (1 C may be evaporated milk)
* 1/2 C sugar
* 1/2 C seedless raisins (optional)
* dash of nutmeg
* 1/4 1 salt

> Mix all ingredients together. Put in baking dish and bake at 300 degrees, stirring occasionally until all milk is absorbed, about 1% hours. Serves 8. Serve with cream and sugar or whipped cream or preferred sauce or topping.
