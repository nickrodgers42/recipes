# Chocolate Dessert
a:Ruth Gibbons

> Melt 1/2 lb marshmallows in double boiler. Add a dash of salt, some green coloring, and 3/4 t almond flavoring. Cool. When it starts to stiffen, fold in 1 pint whipped cream. Line 9 x 13 inch pan with waxed paper. Pat in 1 pkg crushed chocolate wafers. Pour in filling.
> Grate bitter chocolate over top and sprinkle on chopped, unsalted pistachio nuts.
> Serves 10.
