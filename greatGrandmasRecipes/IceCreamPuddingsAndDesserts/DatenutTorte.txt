# Date-nut Torte
a:
* 4 eggs, beaten
* 1 C dry bread crumbs
* 1/2 t salt
* 12 oz dates, chopped
* 1 C sugar
* 1 t baking powder
* 1 t vanilla
* 1 C walnuts, chopped

> Slowly add sugar to eggs and beat until thick and lemon colored. Mix bread crumbs with baking powder and salt. Carefully fold into egg mixture. Add vanilla and spread in a well greased and floured pan. Sprinkle with dates and walnuts. Bake in 350 degree oven 45 to 60 minutes. Serve warm or cold topped with whipped cream.
