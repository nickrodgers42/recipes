# Easy Vanilla Ice Cream
a:
* 1 banana
* 1 egg
* 3 C sugar
* 3 T vanilla
* 2 C milk
* 5 C whipping cream

> Put all but the cream into blender and whirl until sugar is dissolved. Place in 6 quart freezer. Add the cream and enough milk to fill freezer 2/3 full. Do not put cream in the blender. Freeze as usual.
