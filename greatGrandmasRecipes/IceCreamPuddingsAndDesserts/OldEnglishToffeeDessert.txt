# Old English Toffee Dessert
a:Ardella Baugh
* 1 square butter
* 2 C powdered sugar
* 1 heaping T cocoa
* 1 C chopped nuts
* 2 eggs
* 1 t vanilla
* medium sized box vanilla wafers

> Cream butter, sugar, and cocoa. Add eggs one at a time beating well. Add nuts and vanilla. The longer you beat it the better it is. Put half of wafer crumbs in bottom of freezing tray and add the mixture. Put remaining crumbs on top. Freeze. Serve with whipped cream. Very rich but very good.
