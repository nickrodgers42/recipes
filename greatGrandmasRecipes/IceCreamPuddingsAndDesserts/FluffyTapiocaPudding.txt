# Fluffy Tapioca Pudding
a:
* 3 T Minute Brand Tapioca
* 3 T sugar
* 1 egg yolk
* 2 C milk
* 1/8 t salt
* 2 T sugar
* 1 egg white
* 3/4 t vanilla

> Mix tapioca, salt, 4 T sugar, milk and yolk in pan. Let stand 5 minutes. Beat egg white until foamy. Gradually add 2 T sugar, beating to soft peaks; set aside. Cook tapioca mixture over medium heat to a full boil, stirring for 6 to 8 minutes. Gradually add to beaten egg white, stirring quickly until blended. Stir in vanilla. Cool 20 minutes; Stir.
> Serve warm or chilled, plain or with whipped or plain cream and sugar. Is good with many sauces. (I double this amount, it goes fast).
