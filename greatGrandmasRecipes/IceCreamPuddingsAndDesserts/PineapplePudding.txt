# Pineapple Pudding
a:Valera Pond
* 1 pkg banana cream pudding
* 1 1/2 C milk
* 1 C cream, whipped and sweetened
* 8 oz pineapple tidbits, drained
* 8 oz crushed pineapple, drained
* 1 large banana, sliced
* 1/2 C small marshmallows
* 1/4 C maraschino cherries, cut up

> Cook pudding and milk according to pkg directions. Pour into bowl; cover tightly and chill. Remove cover and beat until smooth. Add whipped cream, fruit and marshmallows.
> Chill. Serve in sherbet glasses.
