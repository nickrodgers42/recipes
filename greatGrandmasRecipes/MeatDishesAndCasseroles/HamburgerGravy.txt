# Hamburger Gravy
a:Richard Baugh
@

* 1 lb hamburger
* 1 med onion, chopped
* 1/4 t salt
* 1/8 t pepper
* 1/8 t garlic powder
* 4 T flour

> Brown hamburger and drain. Add onion, salt, pepper and garlic. Saute until onion is tender. Stir in flour until blended. Add milk or water to desired thickness. (approximately 1 quart. Serve with mashed or boiled potatoes and cabbage. Very good with baking powder biscuits, or rice.
