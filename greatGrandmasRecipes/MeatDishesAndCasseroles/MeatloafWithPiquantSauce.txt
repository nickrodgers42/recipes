# Meatloaf With Piquant Sauce
a:
* 2/3 C dry bread crumbs(2 slices bread)
* 1 C milk
* 1 1/2 lbs ground beef
* 2 slightly beaten eggs
* 1/4 C chopped onion
* 1 t salt
* 1/8 t pepper
* 1/2 t sage
* 1 Can cream of mushroom soup
* 1/4 t nutmeg (PIQUANT SAUCE)
* 4 T brown sugar (PIQUANT SAUCE)
* 1 C catsup (PIQUANT SAUCE)
* 1 t dry mustard (PIQUANT SAUCE)

> Soak bread crumbs in milk; add other ingredients and mix well. Form in loaf and cover with sauce. (To make sauce mix the sauce ingredients)
> Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour. Serves 6.
