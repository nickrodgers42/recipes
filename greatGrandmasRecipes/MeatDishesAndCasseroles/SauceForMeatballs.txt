# Sauce For Meatballs:
a:
* 3 8 oz cans tomato sauce
* 1/2 t oregano
* 2 t garlic salt
* 1 6 oz can tomato paste
* 1/2 t marjoram
* 1/2 t dry mustard
* 3/4 C water
* 1/4 t pepper
* 1/2 t celery seed

> 6 C cooked spaghetti or macaroni.(about 3 C un-cooked). Mix and cook over low heat for about 8 minutes. Stir occasionally. Add meatballs, and heat.
