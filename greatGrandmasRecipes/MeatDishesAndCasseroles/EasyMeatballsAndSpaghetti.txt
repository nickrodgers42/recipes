# Easy Meatballs And Spaghetti
a:
* 1 lb ground beef
* 1/2 C chopped onion
* 1/2 lb sliced mushrooms
* 1/4 t pepper
* 1 t salt (to taste)
* 1 #2 1/2 can (3 1/2 C) tomatoes
* 1 clove minced garlic
* 1 t meat extract (optional)
* 1/4 C salad oil
* 8 oz spaghetti
* 1/2 C grated cheese

> Add salt and pepper to meat and form into small balls. Brown in oil. Add other ingredients except spaghetti and cheese. Cover and simmer 1 hour. Cook spaghetti according to pkg directions. To serve, transfer drained spaghetti to large, hot platter, pouring meat sauce over spaghetti. Sprinkle with Italian-style grated cheese. Garnish with green pepper rings. Makes 5 to 6 servings.
