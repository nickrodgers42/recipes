# Enchiladas
a:Bobbie Baugh
* 1 layer crumbled Fritos
* 1 layer chili without beans
* 1 layer finely chopped onions, pre-fried
* 1 layer shredded cheese

> In a well greased, 10 inch flat baking dish, put the ingredients down in layers.
> Repeat; Bake at 325 degrees 45 minutes to 1 hour, covered. Do not brown or cook hard.
