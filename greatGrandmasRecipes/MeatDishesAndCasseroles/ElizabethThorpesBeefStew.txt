# Elizabeth Thorpe's Beef Stew
a:
* 2 lbs stew meat
* 3 medium carrots, sliced bite size
* 1 onion chopped
* 3 potatoes
* 1 bay leaf
* 1 can peas (add at last part of cooking)
* 1 can water
* 1/4 C pickle juice
* 1 t salt
* pepper

> Put in casserole or covered pan and cook 5 hours in 275 degree oven.
