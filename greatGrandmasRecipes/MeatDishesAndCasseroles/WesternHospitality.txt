# Western Hospitality
a:Kae Lynn Beecher
* 1 large can pork and beans
* 1 can tomato sauce (small)
* 1 lb ground beef
* 1/2 C brown sugar
* 2 T onions (minced)
* 1 C chopped carrots

> Cook carrots and brown ground beef. Combine all ingredients in a large casserole dish and bake 30 to 45 minutes at 350 degrees.
