# Thai Dipping Sauce For Thai-style Barbecued Ribs
a:
@

* 2 t fresh lime juice
* 2 t rice wine vinegar
* 1 T soy sauce
* 1 T Hot Thai Chili Sauce or 1 t red pepper
* 1 t minced garlic
* 1/2 t sesame oil
* 3/4 T sugar

> Combine all ingredients and stir until sugar dissolves. Serve with Thai-Style Spare ribs.
