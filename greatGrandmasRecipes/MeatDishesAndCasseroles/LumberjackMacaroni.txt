# Lumberjack Macaroni
a:
* 1/2 lb macaroni
* 2 C cheese grated (1/2 lb)
* 2 to 4 T worcestershire sauce
* 1/4 C chili sauce
* 1/2 C melted butter
* 1/4 C Half and Half, heated

> Cook macaroni as instructed. Drain. Heat large platter very hot Spread macaroni on platter, sprinkle with cheese and other ingredients. Mix with two forks until sauce is creamy. Serve at once on hot plates. Serves 6.
