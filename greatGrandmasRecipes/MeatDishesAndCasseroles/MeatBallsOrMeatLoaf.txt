# Meat Balls Or Meat Loaf
a:Elizabeth Thorpe
* 1 lb ground beef
* 1 egg unbeaten
* 1/2 C cracker or bread crumbs
* 1 medium onion
* 1 can tomato soup or 8 oz tomato sauce
* 1/4 C milk
* 3 T chopped onions
* 1/2 t salt
* 1/4 t sage
* dash of oregano
* 1 t garlic
* salt butter

> Combine meat, egg, crumbs, onion and milk. Form into Ioaf. Cook onions in butter until soft. Add soup and 1/4 C water and 2 T chopped parsley. Heat together and pour over meat. Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes. Put cheese triangles on top and bake until cheese melts. Slice and serve
