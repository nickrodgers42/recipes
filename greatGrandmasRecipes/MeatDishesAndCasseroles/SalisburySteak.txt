# Salisbury Steak
a:
* 1 lb hamburger
* 1 egg
* 1/4 C chopped onion
* 1/3 C milk
* 1 can cream of mushroom soup
* 2/3 C crushed cornflakes or bread crumbs
* 1 t salt
* dash pepper, flour, fat

> Combine all but the soup; form into patties; roll in flour, brown in fat. Drain off extra fat.
> Cover with soup. Cover and simmer 10 minutes or until ready to serve.
