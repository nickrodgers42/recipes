# Round Steak In Mushroom Gravy
a:

> Cut round steak in sewing size pieces. Trim off fat and brown lightly on both sides. Place in casserole. Heat 1 can mushroom soup in the skillet that the meat was browned in. Add 3/4 to 1 can water and stir to loosen brownings from bottom of skillet. Pour over meat.
> Cover and bake in 300 degree oven for 2 1/2 to 3 hours. Add a little more water if needed while roasting. Cooking at a low heat makes the meat very tender.
