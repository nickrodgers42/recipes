# Mustard Ring
a:
* 4 eggs well beaten
* 3/4 C water
* 3 t dry mustard
* 1 C weak vinegar
* 3/4 C sugar
* 1 envelope unflavored gelatin dissolved in 1/4 C water

> Combine all ingredients and cook in double boiler until creamy and smooth. Cool and fold in 1 C whipped cream. Pour into ring mold and refrigerate until set.
