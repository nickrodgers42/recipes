# Beef With Vegetables-family Style
a:
* 2 lbs beef stew meat
* 1/3 C flour
* 2 t salt
* 1/4 t pepper
* 1 clove garlic chopped
* 1/4 C lard or drippings
* 1 quart tomatoes
* 1 T Worcestershire sauce
* 1/4 t cloves
* 6 carrots cut up
* 4 onions quartered
* 1 can peas
* 2 C cut up cabbage

> Combine flour, salt and pepper, and dredge meat. Brown meat on all sides in lard. Pour off drippings. Drain tomatoes, reserving juice. Add tomato juice, Worcestershire sauce, cloves and garlic. Cover tightly and simmer 1/2 hours. Add carrots, cabbage and onions and continue cooking 1 hour. Add tomatoes and peas. Cook 15 minutes more or until meat is tender and vegetables are done. Serves 6 to 8.
