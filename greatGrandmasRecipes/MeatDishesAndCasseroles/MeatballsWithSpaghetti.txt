# Meatballs With Spaghetti
a:
* 1/2 C chopped onion
* 3 T fat
* 2 1/2 C tomatoes
* 2 6 oz cans tomato paste
* 2 C water
* 8 oz spaghetti, cooked as instructed
* 1/2 t pepper
* 1 lb hamburger
* 1/4 t garlic powder or 1 clove garlic minced
* 1/2 t salt
* 1/2 to 1 C dry bread crumbs
* 1 t sugar
* 1/2 milk
* 1 t salt
* 1/2 C grated parmesan cheese

> Brown onion in hot fat; add tomatoes, tomato paste, water and seasonings. Simmer1 hour. Combine remaining ingredients except spaghetti and cheese. Form in balls, roll in flour and brown in hot fat. Add balls to sauce and cook over low heat 15 to 30 minutes.
> Serve sauce over hot spaghetti, sprinkle cheese on top. Makes 6 to 8 servings. 1/4 C sliced mushrooms andlor a little chopped green pepper make good additions to the sauce.
