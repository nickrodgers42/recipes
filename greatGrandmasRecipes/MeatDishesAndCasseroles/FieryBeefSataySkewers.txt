# Fiery Beef Satay Skewers
a:Richard Baugh
@

* 1 1/2 lbs boneless beef, 1 1/2 inch thick
* cherry tomatoes
* green onions
* potatoes
* green pepper
* other vegetables
* 1/2 C Dijon Mustard
* 1/2 C soy sauce 2
* 1/4 C honey
* 2 T fresh lime juice
* 4 t minced garlic
* 1 T ground Thai chili pepper

> Cut vegetables and beef into 1 inch squares and pre-cooked until nearly done.
> In a large bowl, combine marinade ingredients. Remove and reserve 1/2 C for basting.
> Trim fat from beef and cut into 1 to 1 1/2 inch cubes. Add beef to remaining marinade and toss to coat. Cover and marinate in refrigerator for several hours.
> Remove beef from marinade and discard marinade. Altemately thread an equal amount of vegetables and beef onto skewers. Place skewers on grid over medium, ashâ€”covered coals. Grill, uncovered, 10 to 12 minutes for medium doneness. basting frequently with reserved 1/2 C marinade and turning occasionally. Serves 4.
