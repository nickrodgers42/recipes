# Belgian Meatballs
a:
* 1 lb hamburger
* 2 T chopped onion
* 2 T chopped green pepper
* 1/4 C cornmeal
* 1 1/2 C cooked tomatoes
* 1 t chili powder
* 1 1/2 t dry mustard
* 1/8 t pepper
* 1 t salt
* 1/2 C milk
* 1 egg
* 1/4 C flour
* 1/4 C shortening

> Combine all but fat, flour and tomatoes. Form 12 balls; roll in flour and brown in hot fat.
> Add remaining flour and tomatoes. Cover and bake in 450 degree oven for 35 to 45 minutes. Remove meat, pour off fat, and add 1/2 C catsup, 1/2 C hot water, 2 T Worcestershire sauce, and 1/2 t salt. Bring to a boil and serve over meatballs. Serves 6.
