# Spicy Beef Short Ribs
a:
* 2 lbs beef short ribs
* 3 T fat or drippings
* 1 medium onion sliced
* 2 T butter
* 2 T vinegar
* 1 T brown sugar
* 1/2 C catsup
* 1 T Worcestershire sauce
* 1 t prepared mustard
* 1/2 C water
* 1/2 C chopped celery
* 1 t salt and pepper

> Brown short ribs in fat. Brown sliced onion in butter. Add all ingredients except short ribs and simmer until slightly thickened, about 30 minutes. Pour off drippings from short ribs.
> Pour the sauce over the short ribs. Cover and simmer about 2 hours or until meat is tender. 4 to 6 servings.
