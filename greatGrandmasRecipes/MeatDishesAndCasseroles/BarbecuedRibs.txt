# Barbecued Ribs
a:
* 8 or 10 country style spare ribs
* 1 C catsup
* 1 C water
* 2 T prepared mustard
* 1/4 C lemon juice
* 1 t celery salt
* 1/2 t nutmeg
* 2 T brown sugar
* 2 T vinegar
* 4 T Worcestershire sauce

> Brown the ribs and place in a casserole or baking dish. Mix other ingredients; heat and pour over ribs. Bake at 325 degrees for 3 hours. This is also good prepared in a slow cooker.
