# Ham Loaf
a:
* 1 lb ham ground
* 4 slices white bread
* 1 t baking powder
* 2 onions finely chopped
* 2 lbs veal ground
* 1 pint milk
* 2 beaten eggs

> Soak bread in milk; add other ingredients; salt and pepper to taste, and bake in loaf pan at 350 degrees 1% to 2 hours. Serve with mustard sauce or horseradish sauce. Beef or lean pork may be substituted for veal. Tomato juice or diluted tomato soup may be substituted for milk.
