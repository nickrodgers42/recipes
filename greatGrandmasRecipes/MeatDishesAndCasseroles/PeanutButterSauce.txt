# Peanut Butter Sauce:
a:
@

* 1 C peanut butter
* 1 C coconut milk
* 1 T lime juice
* 4 T soy sauce
* 1 T worcestershire sauce
* 6 to 10 drops Tabasco
* 1/4 t salt

> Mix all ingredients and refrigerate. Allow to stand long enough for flavors to blend.
> Warm before serving. Sauce may be thinned with lime juice, coconut milk, or water.
