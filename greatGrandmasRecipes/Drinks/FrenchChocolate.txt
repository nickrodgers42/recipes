# French Chocolate
a:
* 2 1/2 squares un-sweetened chocolate
* 3/4 C sugar
* 6 C hot milk
* 1/2 C cold water
* salt - vanilla
* 1/2 C cream whipped

> Grate chocolate into water and place over low heat, stirring until warm and blended. Add sugar and salt and boil 4 minutes. Fold into whipped cream. Pour 1 rounded T syrup into each serving cup and pour scalded milk over it, filling the cup. Stir to blend and serve at once. Makes 8 to 10 cups. Syrup recipe can be doubled, and refrigerated. Add hot milk when needed.
