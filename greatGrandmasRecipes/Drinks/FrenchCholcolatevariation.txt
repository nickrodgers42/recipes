# French Cholcolate (variation)
a:
* 3 squares unsweetened chocolate or 3 oz chocolate flavor ingredient
* 2 tall cans evaporated milk
* 2/3 C sugar
* 2 C water
* 1 t vanilla

> Slowly cook, chocolate, sugar and water, stirring until well blended. Add vanilla and milk.
> Heat to serving temperature. Do not boil. Makes 6 servings.
