# Glenda's Drink
a:Michelle Baugh
* 2 C sugar
* 2 C water
* 2 lemons
* 2 oranges
* 1 15 oz can crushed pineapple
* 1 diced or mashed banana

> Boil sugar and water; cool. Add juice of lemons and oranges. Freeze. When ready to serve, beat well and pour 7-Up over each serving. Make it thin enough to drink.
