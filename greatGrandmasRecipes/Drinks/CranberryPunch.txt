# Cranberry Punch
a:
* 2 quarts cranberry juice
* 2 quarts apple juice or cider
* 1 T grated orange peel
* 1 T grated lemon peel
* 2 trays ice cubes
* 12 whole cleves
* 1/4 C lemon juice
* 2 C sugar

> Heat cranberry juice, cloves, sugar and grated peels; simmer 5 minutes. Cool and strain.
> Add remaining ingredients. Serve cold.
