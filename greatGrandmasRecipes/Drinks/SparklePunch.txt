# Sparkle Punch
a:
* 2/3 C sugar
* 4 C water
* 2/3 C lemon juice
* 1 6 oz cans frozen orange juice
* 1 6 oz frozen pineapple juice
* 1 6 oz can frozen lime juice
* 1 bottle ginger ale

> Boil sugar and water; cool by adding ice cubes. Dilute frozen juices according to directions; add with lemon juice to syrup. Chill. When ready to serve, add ginger ale.
> Makes about 1 gallon.
