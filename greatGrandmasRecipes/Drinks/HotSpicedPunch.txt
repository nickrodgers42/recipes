# Hot Spiced Punch
a:
* 2 C sugar
* 1 t whole cloves
* 2 quarts water
* 1 stick cinnamon
* 1 6 oz can each frozen orange juice
* 1 6 oz frozen lemon juice
* 1 6 oz can frozen pineapple juice

> Mix juices as directed on can. Boil together sugar, water and spices. Strain. Add frozen juices and water. Heat and serve. Makes 10 servings.
