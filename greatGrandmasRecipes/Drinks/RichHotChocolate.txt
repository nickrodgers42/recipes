# Rich Hot Chocolate
a:
* 1 6 oz. pkg (1 C) semi-sweet chocolate pieces
* 1 1/2 C heavy cream
* 1 C water
* dash salt
* 1 1/2 C milk

> Heat chocolate, water and salt in top of double boiler, (moderate heat), until melted and well blended. Bring to a boil stirring constantly. Place over hot water. Add cream and milk; heat to scalding. Beat with rotary beater until foamy. Serve at once. Makes 6 servings.
