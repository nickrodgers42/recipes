# Banana-lime Ecstacy
a:
* 1/3 C fresh lime juice
* 1/2 large ripe banana
* 3 T confectioners sugar
* 2 ice cubes

> Put all ingredients into a chilled electric blender container. Cover and blend on high speed about 1 minute. Pour into an ice-filled glass. 1 serving. Increase or decrease sugar according to taste.
