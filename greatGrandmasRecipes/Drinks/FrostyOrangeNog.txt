# Frosty Orange Nog
a:
* 3 C orange juice chilled
* 1/4 C sugar
* 1/2 t cinnamon
* 1/4 1 cloves
* 1/4 C lemon juice chilled
* 6 eggs
* 1/4 t ginger

> Combine and beat ingredients with rotary beater
> Stir in 5 C orange juice chilled. Just before serving, spoon in 1 quart vanilla ice cream and 1 quart ginger ale, chilled. Blend with rotary beater. Gamish with nutmeg. Makes 1 gallon (Plain egg nog: Combine 1 well beaten egg, 1 pint milk, sugar to taste, and vanilla. Serve cold).
