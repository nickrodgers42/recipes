# Raspberry Pineapple Punch
a:
* 2 46 oz cans pineapple juice
* 2 small cans M.C.P. lemon juice
* 2 gallons water
* 2 pkg raspberry Kool Aid
* 4 C sugar

> Combine ingredients and chill. Serves 50.
> After about 20 of my piano students performed in a recital for families and friends in the 6th Ward Relief Society room, a few years ago, I served this punch with cookies and candy.
