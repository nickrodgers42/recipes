# Strawberry Smoothie
a:
* 1 carton(8 oz) plain yogurt
* 1/4 C skim milk
* 3 packets Equal sweetener
* 3 C frozen strawberries
* 1 C ice cubes

> Combine yogurt, milk, and Equal, in blender. With blender running, add berries, a few at a time. Blend until smooth. Continue blending and add ice cubes, one at a time, until slushy. Serves 4.
