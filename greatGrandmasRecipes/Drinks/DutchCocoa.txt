# Dutch Cocoa
a:
* 1/2 C cocoa
* 2/3 C sugar
* 1/8 t salt
* 3/4 C water
* 2 C water
* 2 tall cans evaporated milk
* 1 t vanilla

> Heat together sugar, cocoa, salt and 3/4 C water over medium heat, stirring constantly; boil 2 minutes. Add 2 C water, evaporated milk and vanilla. Heat to serving temperature.
> Do not boil. Makes 6 servings.
