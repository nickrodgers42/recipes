# Sweetened Condensed Milk
a:
* 2 C sugar
* 1 C boiling water
* 1/2 C margarine
* 3 1/2 C dry milk solids

> Dissolve sugar in boiling water. Remove from heat and add margarine. Stir until melted.
> Add dry milk gradually, beating with electric beater. Store in covered container in refrigerator up to 4 weeks. Makes 3 cups.
