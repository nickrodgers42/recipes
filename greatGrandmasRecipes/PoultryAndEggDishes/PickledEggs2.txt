# Pickled Eggs
a:Cherrill Nielsen
* 1 t salt
* 1 t mixed spices
* 2 T sugar
* 2 C vinegar
* 12 to 16 hard boiled eggs peeled

> Add sugar, spices and salt to vinegar (dilute 1/3 with water if strong). Simmer about 8 minutes. Strain and pour over eggs arranged in a quart jar. Seal and refrigerate 2 days before eating.
