# Hamburger Casserole For Crockpot
a:
* 2 large potatoes, sliced
* 3 medium carrots, sliced
* 1 #2 can peas, drained
* 3 medium onions, sliced
* 1 1/2 lbs ground beef, browned
* 2 stalks celery, sliced
* 1 10 oz can tomato soup
* 1 can water

> Place layers in the crockpot and season each layer of vegetables with salt and pepper.
> Put the browned meat on top of the oelery. Mix the soup with the water and pour into the pot. Cover and set at low for 6 to 8 hours or high for 2 to 4 hours, stirring occasionally.
> Ingredients may be increased by half for the 4 1/2 quart model crockpot.
