# Chicken Cordon Bleu
a:Isobel Nelson
* 4 chicken breasts (bone, skin, pound)
* 1 C flour
* 2 eggs, beaten
* 4 slices thin boiled ham
* 1 can cream of chicken soup
* 4 slices Swiss cheese
* 1/2 pint sour cream (not IMO)
* 1 pkg chicken Shake and Bake
* 1 pkg bleu cheese dressing (dry)
* 1/4 C grated parmesan cheese

> Roll boned, skinned and pounded chicken breasts and ham and Swiss cheese in 4 rolls.
> Use 2 toothpicks to hold each roll. Dip each in flour then in beaten egg. then in Shake and Bake (Shake and Bake, blue cheese dressing, parmesan cheese).
> Fry in ample oil until light brown. Place in baking dish. Pour 1 can cream of chicken soup over this, then put sour cream on each roll. Sprinkle with parsley flakes.
> Cover with foil or lid and bake at 325 degrees for 1% hours. Serves 4.
