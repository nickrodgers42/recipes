# Hot Chicken Dinner
a:Ruby Lundberg
* 2 1/2 C soft bread crumbs
* 1 large egg or 2 medium eggs
* 1 1/4 C evaporated milk
* 2 1/4 C cheese potato chips broken
* 1 large onion, chopped
* 3 lb C out up chicken
* 1 1/2 C chicken broth
* Salt-pepper to taste

> Mix together and bake at 350 degrees for 40 to 50 minutes. Serves 8.
