# Pickled Eggs
a:
* 3/4 C drained juice from canned beets
* 3/4 C vinegar
* 1/4 C brown sugar
* 1/2 t salt
* 12 whole cloves (or less)
* 6 hard boiled eggs, peeled

> Boil liquids and seasonings. Cool. Pour over eggs in a quart jar. Refrigerate. For optimum eating quality, use within 2 days after preparation.
