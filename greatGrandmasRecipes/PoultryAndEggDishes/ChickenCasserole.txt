# Chicken Casserole
a:Mabel Murray
* 2 C diced chicken
* 1 C diced celery
* 2 C cooked rice
* 1 T lemon juice
* 1 C mushrooms, canned or fresh
* 1 can cream of chicken or mushroom soup
* 1 C sliced water chestnuts
* 1 T chopped onion
* 1/2 1 salt
* 3/4 C real mayonnaise

> Mix all together and put into buttered casserole. Top with buttered crushed oomflakes and slivered almonds. Bake 35 minutes at 350 degrees. Serves 12. Add 1/2 C canned milk if it seems too dry.
