# Moist Dressing For Chicken Or Turkey
a:
* 1 loaf or 8 C stale bread crumbs
* 1 1/2 to 2 T sage
* 1 lb butter
* 8 C chopped onions, cooked slightly
* 2 t baking powder (optional)
* 1 T salt

> Mix together and add enough water to make it quite moist, but not dripping.
> Salt and pepper inside cavity of chicken or turkey and add dressing, not packing in tightly. Close opening by sewing or with skewers. Tie legs together.
> Brush turkey with butter. Roast by preferred method.
