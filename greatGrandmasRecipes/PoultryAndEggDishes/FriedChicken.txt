# Fried Chicken
a:Prakong Baugh
* 2 3 lb chickens cut up
* 2 1/2 T salt (RUB)
* 2 T sugar(RUB)
* 1 t pepper (RUB)
* 1/2 clove garlic minced or 1/8 t garlic powder (RUB)
* 2 beaten eggs (BATTER)
* 1 1/4 C water  (BATTER)
* 2 C flour  (BATTER)
* 1 1/4 t black pepper (BATTER)
* 1 t baking powder  (BATTER)
* 1 1/2 t sugar (BATTER)
* 1 t salt (BATTER)

> Rub well with a mixture of the ingredients for the rub
> Cover and refrigerate overnight. When ready to use, completely coat individual pieces with the mixture fo the ingredients for the batter
> Deep fry in medium hot oil until done and well browned.
