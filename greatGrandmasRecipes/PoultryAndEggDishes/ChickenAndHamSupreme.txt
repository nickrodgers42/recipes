# Chicken And Ham Supreme
a:
* 12 half chicken breasts, boned
* 1/4 C oil or margarine
* 2 C chopped onions
* 1/4 C cornstarch or flour
* 1/2 t salt
* 1/4 t pepper
* 4 C milk
* 3 C water
* 1 C cider or other fruit juice
* 12 chicken bouillon cubes
* 1 1/2 lbs cooked, drained spaghetti
* 12 slices Swiss cheese
* 12 slices boiled ham

> In heavy skilled brown chicken in oil about 15 minutes until tender. Remove chicken.
> Add onions; cook 5 minutes. Mix starch, salt and pepper. Gradually stir in cider until smooth. Add onions with next 3 ingredients; stim'ng constantly and bring to a boil over medium heat. Boil 1 minute. Grease 6 quart shallow baking pan or 2 3 quart casseroles.
> Add spaghetti, pour on sauce and toss. Roll cheese inside ham. Arrange with chicke over spaghetti. Bake tightly covered in 325 degree oven for 45 to 60 minutes or until hot.
> Makes 12 servings. (Or prepare the day before but do not bake. Cover, refrigerate. To serve bake 1 1/2 to 1 3/4 hours).
