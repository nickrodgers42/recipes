# Dilled Eggs
a:
* 1 1/2 C white vinegar
* 1 C water
* 3/4 t dill
* 1/4 t white pepper
* 3 tsp salt
* 1/4 t mustard seed
* 1/2 t onion juice
* 1/2 t minced gariic

> Bring the mixture to near boiling and simmer for 5 minutes. POUR over peeled, hard boiled eggs in a jar, seal and refrigerate to season.
