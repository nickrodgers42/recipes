# Chicken Noodle Casserole
a:Ida Harris

> 1 chicken cooked, cut into pieces
> 1 can chunk tuna
> 1 can cream of chicken soup
> 2 1/4 C top milk
> 1 can cream of mushroom soup
> 2 T flour
> 1/4 Cchopped pimento
> 6 T butter
> 1/2 Green pepper, chopped
> 1 C chopped celery
> 1 C chopped onion
> 2 T chopped parsley
> 1/2 lb cooked, drained noodles
> Cook onion, celery and green pepper in butter until partly cooked. Stir in flour and milk; heat to boiling. Mix all other ingredients and add to chicken and tuna. Add milk and vegetable mixture. Mix well and put into buttered 2 quart casserole. Top with buttered bread crumbs. Bake for 35 minutes at 350 degrees, or until hot through.
