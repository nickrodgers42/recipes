# Stuffed Pork Chqps
a:
* 6 pork chops
* 2 T butter or margarine, melted
* 2 T chopped onion
* 1/4 t sage
* 1/4 t crushed basil leaves
* 1 T parsley flakes salt-pepper

> Cut along bone about 1/2 way through, then toward outside to make a pocket in  pork chops 1 inch thick or two thin chops may be held together with toothpicks.
> Saute until onion is golden. Toss with 1 1/2 C small dry bread cubes. Stuff mixture into pork chop pockets. Brown in a small amount of fat, then place in a 13 x 9 x 2 inch baking pan or casserole. Sprinkle with 1/4 C onion soup mix. Pour 1/2 C water over chops, cover and bake at 325 degrees about 1 hour or until tender. Do not over-bake, or it will be dry. Makes 6 servings.
