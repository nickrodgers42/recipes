# Mushroom And Chicken Stuffed Crepes
a:
* 12 oz canned (1 lb) fresh mushrooms
* 6 T butter
* 1/3 C flour
* 1/2 C boiling water
* 2 egg yolks
* 1/2 t salt
* 1/4 t pepper
* 2 chicken bouillon cubes
* 1 C light cream or Half and Half
* 1/2 t nutmeg
* 4 C diced cooked chicken (crab meat may be used in place of chicken. Both go well with mushrooms.)

> Rinse, pat dry and slice fresh mushrooms or drain canned mushrooms. Set aside. In medium saucepan melt 4 T butter. Add half of the mushrooms; saute for 2 minutes. Stir in flour; stir and cook for 1 minute. Dissolve bouillon cubes in hot water. Blend into flour mixture. Cook and stir until mixture thickens. Lightly beat egg yolks with cream, salt and pepper, Stir into mixture in saucepan; cook and stir over low heat until hot, do not boil.
> Remove from heat and pour half the sauce into medium bowl. Stir in chicken and nutmeg. Spoon 1/2 C of the chicken mixture onto each crepe; roll up crepes and place, seam side down in a 13 x 9 x 2 inch baking pan. In small skillet melt remaining 2 T butter. Add remaining mushrooms and saute for 2 minutes. Stir into sauce left in saucepan. Spoon over crepes. Cover and bake at 375 degrees until hot, about 20 minutes. Makes 6 servings, or 12 crepes.
