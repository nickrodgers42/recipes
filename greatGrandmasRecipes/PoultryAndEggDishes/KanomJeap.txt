# Kanom Jeap
a:Achara Anukularmphai
* 1 t vinegar
* 1 C fresh ground pork
* 3 mushrooms, finely chopped
* 1/4 t MSG
* 1 green onion finely chopped
* 1 T soy sauce
* 2 t sugar
* 1/4 t pepper
* 2 T water
* 1 T chopped parsley
* 1 T cornstarch
* 1/4 t season all
* 1 egg white
* 1 t cooking oil
* 20 to 25 wanton Skins
* salt to taste

> Mix all ingredients except wanton skins. Put about 1 rounded T of the mixture on each wanton skin and wrap well. Steam about 20 minutes. Grease steaming plate with vegetable oil. Fry fresh chopped garlic in vegetable oil and sprinkle over each cooked wanton. Dip or pour Worcestershire sauce over each Kanom Jeap as it is eaten.
