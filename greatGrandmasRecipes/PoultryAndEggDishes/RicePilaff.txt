# Rice Pilaff
a:Janice Nielsen
* 1 C long grain rice
* 1 stick butter
* 3 medium onions, chopped
* 1 can sliced mushrooms
* 2 cans beef consomme

> Brown rice and onions in butter, slowly and lightly. Add beef broth and mushrooms. Salt to taste. Cover and bake in 1 1/2 quart casserole until liquid is absorbed, about 1 hour at 350 degrees. Serves 8 to 10.
