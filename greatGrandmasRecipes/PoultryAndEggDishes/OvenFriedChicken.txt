# Oven Fried Chicken
a:
* 1 C flour
* 2 t salt
* 1 t paprika
* 1 t baking powder
* 1/4 t pepper

> Dip chicken pieces in 1 beaten egg and 1/2 C condensed milk. Then roll in the mixture of the ingredients
> In greased cookie sheet or shallow pan, melt 3/4 stick margarine. Place chicken pieces in pan. Bake 1/2 hour at 370 degrees. Turn and bake A hour more. It's very close to real fried chicken.
