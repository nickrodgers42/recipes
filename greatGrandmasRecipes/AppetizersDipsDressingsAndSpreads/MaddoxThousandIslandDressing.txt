# Maddox Thousand Island Dressing
a:Vemetta Earl
* 1/2 C Campbells Tomato Soup
* 1/4 C sugar
* 1 t onion juice
* 1/4 C vinegar
* 1 t paprika
* 2 1/2 t salt
* 3/4 C salad oil
* 1 T worcestershire sauce
* 1/2 t dry mustard
* 1 1/3 C Miracle Whip

> Mix dry ingredients first. Then add everything but the Miracle Whip. Beat until smooth , then Blend in the Miracle Whip.
