# Cheese Spread
a:
* 4 oz cream cheese
* 1 t grated parmesan cheese
* 1 C cottage cheese
* 1 t paprika
* 3 green onions finely chopped
* 1 t prepared, french mustard
* 1 generous pinch of caraway seeds

> Mash and mix until smooth. Pack well in jar or bowI. Cover and ripen in refrigerator at least 2 days before serving.
> Serve with crackers or chips, and tomato juice cocktail.
