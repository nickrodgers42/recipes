# Five Alarm Salsa
a:
@

* 1 28 oz can whole tomatoes
* 2 jalapeno peppers, seeded and chopped
* 1/2 C onion, chopped
* 2 cloves gartic, minced
* 2 T fresh lime juice
* 1/4 C cilantro, chopped
* 1/4 t black pepper

> In a medium saucepan, bring all ingredients to a boil and then simmer for 15 minutes.
> This recipe can be multiplied and bottled according to USDA directions.
> The latest USDA directions can be obtained from your county extension office.
