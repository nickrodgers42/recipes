# French Dressing
a:
* 1 clove garlic crushed
* 1/2 C vinegar
* 1/2 C catsup
* 1/2 t dry mustard
* 1 T sugar

> Mix ingredients in a shaker, shake until blended. Chill. Good on salads, mixed greens,
> vegetabies, meat or fish. Shake well before using. Makes 1 pint.
> For creamy dressing add 1/4 C evaporated milk. For thicker dressing, add 1/4 C sour cream.
