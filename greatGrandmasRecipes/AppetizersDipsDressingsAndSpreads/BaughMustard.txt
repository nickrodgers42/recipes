# Baugh Mustard
a:Ardella Baugh
@

* 5 t dry mustard
* scant 1/4 t tumeric
* 5 t flour
* 1/4 C vinegar
* 1 t salt
* 1 1/2t sugar
* warm water

> Mix dry ingredients and add enough warm water to make paste. Add vinegar and cook until thickened. After cooking allow to cool and store in refrigerator. Keep adding water to maintain desired consistency.
