# Mushroom Sauce
a:
* 1 C sliced mushrooms (4 oz can)
* 1 t worcestershire sauce
* 2 T butter
* 1/4 t salt
* 1/2 t paprika
* 1/2 C milk
* 1/2 C dairy sour cream
* 2 T flour

> Saute mushrooms in butter for 3 minutes and stir in flour and seasonings. Add milk and worcestershire sauce. Cook stirring until thick. Add cream and heat again just before serving over hot cooked vegetables. Makes 1 1/3 cup.
