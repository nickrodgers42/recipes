# Salad Dressing
a:Barbara Bradley
* 1 C vinegar
* 1 C water
* 1 C sugar
* 4 T flour
* 1/2 t salt
* 1 egg

> Bring water and vinegar to a boil and pour over a mixture sugar, flour, and salt. Stir until smooth. Allow to cool. Add egg and heat again untiI mixture thickens. Add a littIe butter or cream and mix well. Store in refrigerator.
