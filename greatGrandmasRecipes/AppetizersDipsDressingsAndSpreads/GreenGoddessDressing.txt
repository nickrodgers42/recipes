# Green Goddess Dressing
a:
* 1 1/4 C mayonnaise
* 2 T lemon juice or tarragon vinegar
* 4 sprigs parsley, snipped.
* 1/2 clove garlic, minced
* 2 green onions, including tops minced
* 1/2 C sour cream
* 1 dash pepper

> Combine all ingredients. mix well. Store in refrigerator. This is very good on tossed salad and cooked green vegetables.
