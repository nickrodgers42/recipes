# Oriental Marinade For Chicken Or Pork
a:
@

* 1/2 C soy sauce
* 1/4 C Hoi Sin sauce
* 1/4 C rice wine
* 6 cloves garlic, minced
* 2 T sugar
* 3 green onions, sliced(include green part)
* 1 t crushed hot red pepper
* 1 T sesame oil

> Mix all ingredients and pour over meat. Make certain that all is coated. Marinate several hours, turning frequently. Hoi Sin Sauce can be purchased in oriental markets.
