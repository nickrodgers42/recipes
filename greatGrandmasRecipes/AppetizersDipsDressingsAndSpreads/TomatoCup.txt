# Tomato Cup
a:Emma Jean Maughan
* 1/4 C butter
* 1/2 t garlic salt
* 36 oz tomato juice
* 1 T chopped parsley
* 3 T grated parmesan cheese
* 5 t sugar
* 2 stalks celery, blended
* 1 bay leaf (optional)
* 36 oz beef broth

> Cook onion in butter until tender; add other ingredients. Simmer for thirty minutes.
> Makes about 6 pints. Serve hot.
