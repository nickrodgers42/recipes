# Grapefruit-pineapple Ice Appetizer
a:
* 3/4 C sugar
* 2 C water
* 1 15 oz can grapefruit sections, cut up
* 1 15 oz can crushed pineapple

> Boil sugar and water for 3 minutes, and cool. Add fruits and mix together with rotary beater. Pour into refrigerator tray and freeze until slushy. Serve with cold 7-up poured over the fruit. Slush may need to be beaten smooth before adding 7-up.
> Serves 6.
