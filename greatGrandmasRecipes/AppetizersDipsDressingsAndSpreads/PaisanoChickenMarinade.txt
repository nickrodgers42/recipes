# Paisano Chicken Marinade
a:
@

* 1 C pineapple juice
* 1/3 C soy sauce
* 1 clove garlic, minced
* 1/3 C ltaIian salad dressing

> Combine all ingredients and blend. Pour over chicken that has been cut into pieces, cover and allow to marinate for 2 to 8 hours. Turn frequently. Cook chicken as you want.
