# Roquefort Or Bleu Cheese Salad Dressing
a:
* 1 T chopped onion
* 1/4 C sour cream
* 1/2 C mayonnaise
* 2 T chopped parsley
* 2 T vinegar
* 2 oz Bleu or Roquefort cheese
* 1/2 clove garlic, minced salt and pepper to taste

> Combine vegetables and sour cream, mixing well. Stir in vinegar and lemon juice. Crumble cheese and stir into dressing. Mix well. Add salt and pepper. Roquefort cheese makes the better dressing.
