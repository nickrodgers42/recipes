# Cocktail Niblets
a:
* 1/4 C butter
* 12 to 14 oz mixed nuts with salted peanuts
* 1 t tabasco
* 1 T worcestershire sauce
* 1 C pretzel sticks
* 1 1/2 C Wheat Chex
* 1 1/2 C Corn Chex
* 1 1/2 C Rice Chex
* 1 1/2 Cup Cheerios

> In a large pan meIt butter with seasonings. Pour over dry ingredients and bake uncovered in 225 degree oven for 25 to 30 minutes, until light cereals start to turn brown. Stir every 10 minutes. Serve warm or coId. Store cooled NibIets in tightly closed container. If snack needs re-crisping, reheat in slow oven for a few minutes.
