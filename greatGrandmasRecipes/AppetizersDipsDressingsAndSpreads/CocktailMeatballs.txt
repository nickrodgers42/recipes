# Cocktail Meatballs
a:
@

* 1 lb very lean ground beef
* 1 t grated lemon rind
* 1 large egg, beaten
* 4 t lemon juice
* 1/4 beef broth
* 1 tsalt
* 1/4 t nutmeg
* 1 slice fresh bread, crumbled
* 1/4 t allspice
* 2 T chopped onion

> Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
> Prepare a shallow baking pan with vegetable oil coating, set aside. Combine all ingredients, mix well. Form mixture into tiny balls, measuring 1 level teaspoon per ball. Place balls 1 inch apart on prepared pan and bake 10 minutes.
> Serve on toothpicks.
