# Fruit Cocktail
a:
* 2 C water
* 2 C sugar
* 1 C crushed pineapple
* 1 C orange juice
* 3 bananas, mashed
* 3 Iemons, juiced
* 1 pinch salt

> Dissolve sugar and salt in water, then add the remaining ingredients and freeze in trays. When getting ready to serve blend frozen mixture and spoon into sherbet glasses. Top with 7-up or ginger ale. Garnish with mint or cherry. This is also delicious served in tall glasses with grape juice poured over it.
