# Crab, Grapefruit, And Tomato Juice Cocktail
a:
* 1 15 oz can grapefruit sections drained
* 4 C tomato juice
* 1 6 1/2 oz can crab meat, flaked
* 1 t lemon juice
* 1 dash tabasco

> Combine and chill grapefruit, tomato juice and crabmeat. Before serving add lemon juice and tabasco, mix and put into serving dishes. Serves 6.
