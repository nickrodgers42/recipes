# Tomatillo Salsa
a:
@

* 2 lb fresh tomatillos
* 1 C onion, chopped
* 1 or 2 jalepeno peppers
* 1 C fresh cilantro, chopped
* 1/4 C fresh lemon grass base, minced
* 1/4 C fresh lime juice

> Remove husks from tomatillos, wash and dry. Cut in quarters. Combine tomatillos and remaining ingredients in a steel pan. Heat to boiling, stirring frequently. Reduce heat and simmer, uncovered for 20 to 30 minutes.(Tomatillos look like husk covered green tomatoes and can be purchased in nearly every supermarket.)
