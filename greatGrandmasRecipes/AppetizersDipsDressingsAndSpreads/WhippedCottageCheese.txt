# Whipped Cottage Cheese
a:
@

* 1 C creamed cottage cheese
* 1/8 t salt
* 1/4 C water
* 1 T white vinegar

> Combine all ingredients in a blender, blend at slow speed until smooth. Chili and let stand for at least two hours before serving. Serve cold.
> 
> This is a very good dip base for commercial salad dressing mixes such as Good Seasons, or Hidden Valley Ranch. If you are going to use this as a base for salad dressing mix, bring to room temperature, mix other dressing powders and chill.
