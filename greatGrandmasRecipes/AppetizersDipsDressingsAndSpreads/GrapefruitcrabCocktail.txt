# Grapefruit-crab Cocktail
a:
* 1 6 1/2 oz can crab meat, flaked
* 1 T lemon juice
* 2 C grapefruit segments
* 1 dash Tabasco sauce
* 1 C mayonnaise, or salad dressing
* 8 individual servings torn lettuce
* 2 T catsup

> Chill all ingredients. Sprinkle crabmeat with lemon juice. Alternate torn lettuce, grapefruit, and crab meat in cocktail glasses. Combine remaining ingredients and mix well. Pour over cocktail in glasses.
> Serves 8.
