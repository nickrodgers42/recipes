# Modified Mayonnaise
a:
* 1 egg
* 1 C water
* 1 1/2 t salt
* 2 t prepared mustard
* 3/4 C salad oil
* 1/4 C vinegar
* 2 T sugar
* 1/4 C cornstarch
* 1/8 t paprika

> Put egg, sugar, seasonings, vinegar and oil into mixing bowl, but do not stir. Make a paste of cornstarch and water. Cook over Iow heat until it boils and clears up. Add hot cornstarch mixture gradually to ingredients in mixing bowl, beating briskly. CooI before using. Makes 1 pint.
