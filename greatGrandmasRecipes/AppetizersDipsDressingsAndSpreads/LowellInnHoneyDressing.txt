# Lowell Inn Honey Dressing
a:Louise Baugh
* 1/3 C sugar
* 1 t paprika
* 1/4 t salt
* 1/3 C honey
* 3/4 to 1 C salad oil
* 5 T vinegar
* 1 t dry mustard
* 1 t celery seed
* 1 T lemon juice

> Mix in blender adding oil very slowly. This is very good over grapefruit and orange sections, or any fruit combination. Makes 1 pint.
