# Clamato Dip
a:
* 2 ripe avocados peeled. cut into cubes
* 1 7 1/2 oz can minced clams, drained
* 1 T chopped onion
* 2 T mayonnaise
* 1/2 t pepper
* 1 dash garlic salt
* 1 dash Tabasco sauce
* 1 T lemon juice

> Combine all ingredients and blend untiI smooth. Chill for several hours. Serve with crackers, chips.
> Makes 1 1/2 cups.
