# Pumpkin Butter
a:Tracey Baugh Wright
@

* 4 C cooked pumpkin puree
* 3 1/2 C sugar
* 1 1/2 C honey
* 1 1/2 t cinnamon
* 1/4 t allspice
* 1/2 t salt
* 1 C orange juice

> Combine all ingredients and cook until thickened to desired consistency. Can be bottIed in water-bath for fifteen minutes. Freezes well in margarine tubs.
