# Cooked Salad Dressing
a:
* 4 T sugar
* 2 t salt
* 4 T flour
* 1 1/2 C milk
* 1/2 C mild vinegar
* 1 t dry mustard
* 4 slightly beaten egg yolks or 2 eggs

> Mix dry ingredients, add egg yolks and miIk. Cook in double boiler until thick, stirring constantly. Add vinegar and 1 T butter. Mix well and cool.
