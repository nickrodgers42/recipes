# Easy Country Sauce
a:
* 1 T butter
* 1/2 C American cheese, shredded
* 1/2 t salt
* 1/4 t Tabasco sauce
* 1 C milk
* 2 slices bacon, fried and crumbled

> Melt butter and blend in flour, add milk and heat until boiling and thick stirring constantly.
> Add remaining ingredients and cook for one more minute. Serve immediately on onions, cauliflower, green beans, potatoes, broocoli, peas, or carrots.
> Serves 4 to 6.
