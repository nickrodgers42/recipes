# Spinach Dip
a:
* 1/2 pkg frozen spinach
* 2 C mayonnaise
* 1 1/2 t mono-sodium glutamate (optional)
* 1/2 tsalt
* 1/4 C dried chives
* 1/2 t pepper

> Mix in blender and chill for several hours. This is excellent as a clip for raw carrots, celery, yams, cucumbers, zucchini, green peppers, mushrooms, cauliflower , and parsnips. Also good on crackers and potato chips.
