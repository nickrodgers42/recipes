# Zucchini Bread #2
a:Ida Harris
* 2 C peeled and grated zucchini
* 1 C crushed pineapple, drained
* 5 eggs. beaten
* 1 1/2 C oil
* 3 C sugar
* 4 C flour, sifted with dry ingredients
* 1 1/2 t salt
* 2 t baking powder
* 2 t baking soda
* 1 t nutmeg
* 3 to 4 t cinnamon
* 1/2 to 1 C chopped nuts
* 3 t vanilla

> Mix all ingredients together, grease pans and fill 1/2 full. Bake at 325 degrees for 60 minutes.
> This is a good variation.
