# Raised Muffins
a:
* 1 C milk
* 3/4 t salt
* 1 C boiling water
* 1/4 yeast cake
* 2 T butter
* 1 egg, well beaten
* 1/4 C sugar
* 4 C flour

> Add butter, sugar, and salt to milk and water, cool to lukewarm and add yeast. When yeast is soft add egg and flour stir thoroughly. Cover and let rise over night. In the morning, fill buttered muffin tins two thirds full; let rise until rings are full, and bake at 400 degrees for thirty minutes or until golden brown.
