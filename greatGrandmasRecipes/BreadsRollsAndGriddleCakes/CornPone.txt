# Corn Pone
a:
* 1 C corn meal
* 1/2 t salt
* 1/8 t baking soda
* 1/2 C boiling water
* 1 T salad oil
* 1/3 C sour milk or buttermilk

> Sift the corn meal with salt and baking soda. Add the water. oil and the sour milk to make a soft dough that can be molded with your hands. Shape into 8 flat round cakes and place on well-greased baking sheet. Bake 20 minutes in moderate oven at 350 degrees until golden brown on the bottom.
> Yield 8.
