# Dilly Casserole Bread
a:Ida Harris
* 1 pkg dry yeast
* 2 t dill seed
* 1/4 C warm water
* 1 t salt
* 1 C creamed cottage cheese
* 1/4 t baking soda
* 1 T instant minced onion
* 1 egg, unbeaten
* 2 T sugar
* 2 1/4 C all purpose flour
* 1 T melted butter

> Soften yeast in warm water. Combine cottage cheese, sugar, onion, butter. dill seed, salt, egg and softened yeast. Add flour to form a stiff dough, beating well. Let rise in a warm place until double in bulk, 50 to 60 minutes. Stir down and turn into a well greased round casserole. Let rise until light, 30 to 40 minutes. Bake at 350 degrees for 40 to 50 minutes, until golden brown. Brush with soft butter and sprinkle with salt. (This may be baked in two Ioaf pans.) Slice, butter and wrap in foil and heat as needed.
