# Texas Cornbread
a:
* 1 C yellow cornmeal
* 1/2 C flour
* 1 t baking powder
* 1/2 t salt
* 1 egg
* 1/2 C milk

> Mix all ingredients until batter is smooth. Pour into 9 inch, deep dish pie pan. Preheat oven to 450 degrees, place into oven and turn temperature to 475 degrees.
> Bake for 15 to 20 minutes until golden brown. Serve hot with butter and honey.
