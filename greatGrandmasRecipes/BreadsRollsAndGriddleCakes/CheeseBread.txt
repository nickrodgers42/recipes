# Cheese Bread
a:
* 2 C milk
* 2 T butter
* 3 C grated cheese
* 1/4 C warm water
* 1/4 C sugar
* 5 1/2 C flour
* 1 t salt
* 1 pkg dry yeast, or 1 yeast cake

> Scald milk, add 2 C cheese, sugar and salt. Cool. Add butter, yeast and flour, then remaining cheese. Let rise; punch down and let rise again. Mold in 2 pans, and let rise. Bake 15 minutes at 400 degrees and 25 minutes at 350 degrees or until done.
> Winner in, "queen of the cooks", contest
