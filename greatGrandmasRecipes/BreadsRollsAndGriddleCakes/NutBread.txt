# Nut Bread
a:Elizabeth Thorpe
* 1 T butter
* 1 1/2 t baking soda
* 3 C wheat flour
* 1 1/2 t salt
* 1 C sour milk
* 1 C chopped nuts
* 1/2 C sugar

> Mix all ingredients together and then add 1 more C sour milk. Shape into loaves and bake in greased coffee cans. filling cans 1/2 full. Bake with lids on at 350 degrees for 1 hour.
