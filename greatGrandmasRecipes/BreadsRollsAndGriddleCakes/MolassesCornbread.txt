# Molasses Cornbread
a:
* 1 C wheat flour
* 1 C milk
* 1 C yellow cornmeal
* 1 egg, slightly beaten
* 1/2 t salt
* 1/4 C melted shortening
* 3 t baking powder
* 1/2 C molasses

> Combine dry ingredients and mix together. Combine milk, e99. shortening and molasses and blend. Stir into dry ingredients until just moist. Pour into 9 X 9 X 2 inch square pan, (or equivalent). Bake at 425 degrees for 25 to 30 minutes or until done. Serves 12 easily.
