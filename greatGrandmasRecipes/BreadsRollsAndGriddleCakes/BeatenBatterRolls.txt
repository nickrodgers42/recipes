# Beaten Batter Rolls
a:
* 2 C scalded milk
* 6 C flour
* 1 t salt
* 1/2 C shortening
* 1/4 C sugar
* 3 eggs, well beaten

> Dissolve 3 yeast cakes or 3 pkg dry yeast in 1/2 C warm water with 1 t sugar. Combine remaining ingredients. Stir in yeast and mix well. Let rise twice. Spoon into well greased muffin tins. Let rise. Bake at 375 degrees 12 to 15 minutes. These rolls can be made and baked in about 1 1/2 hours. Makes 3 dozen large rolls.
