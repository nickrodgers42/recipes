# Yum Yum Cake
a:
@

* 1 yellow or white cake, baked and cooled
* 1 large can crushed pineapple, drained
* 8 oz Cool Whip, room temperature
* 1 pkg vanilla instant pudding mixed and cooled
* 1/4 C pecans, chopped
* 1/4 C coconut, roasted
* 8 oz cream cheese, warmed and whipped

> ICING: Mix Cool Whip. vanilla pudding, and cream cheese until smooth and blended. Fold in pineapple. Do not stir. Spread on cake and sprinkle with coconut and nuts. Chill.
