# Sour Cream Cake+ (5000 Ft Altitude)
a:
* 1 1/2 C sifted cake flour
* 1 C thin sour cream
* 1 t baking soda
* 1 C minus 2 T sugar
* 1/2 t baking powder
* 2 eggs, well beaten
* 1/2 t salt
* 1 t vanilla

> Sift flour with soda, baking powder and salt 3 times. Beat cream until frothy, add sugar gradually. then eggs and vanilla. Fold in flour, a small amount at a time. Beat after each addition until smooth. Bake in 2 greased 8 inch layer pans at 350 degrees for 25 minutes.
> When cool, spread Fudge Frosting between layers and on top and sides of cake. While frosting is still soft, chopped nuts or coconut may be sprinkled over top and sides of cake, or decorated with walnut halves. (For 3000 ft. altitude, change sugar amount to 1 C.
> For 7500 Ft. altitude, change sugar to 3/4 C.
