# Sponge Cake
a:Pearl Rice

> Beat 6 egg yolks until thick. Add 1 C sugar, 1 T water, 1 T lemon juice, 1 T grated lemon rind. Beat well. Add 1 C sifted cake flour, 1/2 t salt. Beat 6 egg whites until stiff.
> Fold carefully into yolk mixture. Bake in ungreased tube pan at 325 degrees for 45 to 50 minutes.
