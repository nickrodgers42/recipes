# Brown Sugar, Double Boiled Icing
a:
* 1 Large egg white
* 3 T water
* 1 C brown sugar
* 1/4 C raisins, chopped

> Combine egg white. sugar and water in double boiler. Stir just enough to mix well. Place over rapidly boiling water and beat with rotary beater until it will hold shape, about 5 minutes. Remove from heat, fold in raisins carefully. Raisins thin icing, so be sure the mix is cooked enough before adding raisins.
