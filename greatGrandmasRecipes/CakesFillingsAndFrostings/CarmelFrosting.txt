# Carmel Frosting
a:
* 1/2 C Sugar
* 1 C evaporated, condensed milk
* 2 C sugar
* 1 T butter
* 1/2 C water
* 1 T white corn syrup

> Melt 1/2 C sugar in a heavy pan to a nut brown. Add water and stir until all sugar is dissolved. Add remaining ingredients. Stir constantly over medium heat until it comes to a boil. Cook with lid on for 5 minutes, remove lid and cook to soft ball stage. Cool and beat until caramelize. Let stand for 5 minutes, then stir in enough cream to make it of spreading consistency.
