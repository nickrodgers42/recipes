# Date Nut Cake
a:Edna Speth
* 1 C dates chopped
* 1 C boiling water
* 1 t baking soda
* 3 T butter creamed with 1 C sugar
* 1 egg beaten.
* 1 1/2 C flour,
* 1/2 C chopped nuts
* 1 t vanilla

> Pour boiling water over dates and soda and let stand while mixing the butter, sugar, egg. Add four chopped nuts, and vanilla
> Bake at 325 degrees until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.
