# Swedish Jelly Roll
a:
* 4 eggs
* 2 t baking powder
* 1/4 t salt
* 1 C boiling water
* 1 1/2 C sugar
* 2 C sifted flour
* 1 t flavoring

> Beat eggs until thick, add sugar gradually, beating all the time. Add flour, baking powder and salt and beat well. Then add boiling water and beat well. Bake in large paper lined, 15 x 18 inch pan for 10 minutes at 375 degrees. Remove paper, trim edges, and dust with powdered sugar and roll up in damp cloth. When cool. unroll, remove cloth, spread with jelly and roll up again. This may be spread with ice cream softened and put into freezer.
> Slice off as jelly roll and serve with chocolate sauce.
