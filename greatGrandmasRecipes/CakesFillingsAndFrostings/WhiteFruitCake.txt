# White Fruit Cake
a:Lucinda Larsen

> Cream together, 1 C sugar, 1 C shortening, and 4 eggs. Add 1 T lemon juice, 1/2 C pineapple jam. (Cook equal parts of pineapple and sugar until thick), and 1 T fruit flavoring. Sift together 2 C flour, 1/4 1 salt. and 1 1 baking powder. Add to creamed mixture. Then add 1 C dried candied pineapple, 1 1/2 C candied cherries, 1 C blanched, chopped almonds, 1 C coconut (optional). 1 C candied orange pieces. Place in well greased. paper lined loaf pans and bake at 275 degrees for 2 hours. or until firm. This cake will keep for months.
