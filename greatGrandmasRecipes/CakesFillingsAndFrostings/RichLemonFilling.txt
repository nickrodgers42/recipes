# Rich Lemon Filling
a:
* 3/4 C sugar
* 1 egg, well beaten
* 4 T flour
* 2 T butter
* 1 dash of salt
* 1/4 C lemonjuice
* 1/2 C water
* 1/2 t grated lemon rind

> Combine sugar, flour and salt in top of double boiler; add water and egg, mixing thoroughly. Place over boiling water and cook stirring 10 minutes, or until thickened. Add butter, lemon juice and rind. Cool. Makes 1 3/4 C.
