# Oatmeal Cake
a:Cherrill Nielsen
* 1 1/2 C boiling water
* 1 C quick oats
* 1/4 lb butter
* 2 eggs, beaten
* 1 C brown sugar, packed
* 1 t vanilla
* 1 t cinnamon
* 1/4 t salt
* 1 C sugar
* 1 1/3 C flour
* 1 t baking soda
* 1 t nutmeg

> Pour water over oats, add butter and let stand until butter is melted. Add eggs, vanilla and brown sugar.
> Sift together cinnamon, salt, 1 1/3 C sugar, salt, flour, baking soda, and nutmeg.
> Combine all ingredients and bake in 8 X 11 inch, well greased pan at 350 degrees for 35 minutes. Add Topping
> For the topping: mix melted butter, chopped nuts, condensed and evaporated milk, 1/2 C brown sugar, vanilla, shredded coconut, pour over hot cake, and broil until light brown (about 8 minutes)
