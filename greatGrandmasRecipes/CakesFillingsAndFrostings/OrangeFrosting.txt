# Orange Frosting
a:
* Grated rind and juice of 1 orange
* 1 t lemon juice
* 3 T butter
* 2 C powdered sugar sifted with 1/8 t salt
* 1 egg yolk slightly beaten

> Blend butter and sugar thoroughly. Mix juices and egg yolk and add to creamed mixture, beat until light and fluffy. Spread over orange cake or white cake.
