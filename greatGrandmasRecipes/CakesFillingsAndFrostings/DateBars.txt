# Date Bars
a:Leatha Lundahl
* 3 eggs
* 1/8 t salt
* 1 C sugar
* 1 lb dates, cut fine
* 1 C walnuts, chopped
* 2 t vanilla
* 1 1/2 C flour
* 2 t baking powder

> Beat eggs very well, add sugar gradually. then vanilla, dates and nuts, before adding dry ingredients. Spread out in large baking pan well oiled. Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes, or until top springs back when pressed lightly. Cut in bars while warm. May be rolled in powdered sugar.
