# Date Cake
a:Elizabeth Thorpe
* 1 lb dates, cut up
* 1 t baking powder
* 1 lb nuts, chopped
* 4 eggs
* 1 C sugar
* 1 t vanilla
* 1/2 t salt
* maraschino cherries
* 1 C flour

> Beat eggs until very thick, add sugar gradually. Blend in sifted dry ingredients and vanilla.
> Spread over dates, nuts and cherries in greased pan. Bake at 300 degrees for 1 hour and 10 minutes or until done.
