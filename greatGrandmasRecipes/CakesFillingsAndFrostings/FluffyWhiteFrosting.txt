# Fluffy White Frosting
a:Dana Briton
* 1 egg white
* 2/3 C sugar
* 1/4 C light corn syrup
* 2 T water
* 1/4 t salt
* 1/8 t cream of tarter
* 1 t vanilla

> Cook over boiling water beating with rotary beater until mixture stands in peaks. Remove from heat. Add vanilla and continue beating until thick enough to spread. (This is like 7 minute frosting, but a smaller amount.)
