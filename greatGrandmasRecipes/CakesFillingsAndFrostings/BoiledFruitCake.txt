# Boiled Fruit Cake
a:Lucinda Larsen
* 1 C shortening
* 2 C sugar
* 2 C cold water
* 1 pkg (1 lb) seedless raisins
* 1/2 t salt
* 1 pkg (1 lb) seeded raisins, currants or dates
* 1/2 t allspice
* 1 t ground cloves
* 1 t cinnamon

> Boil the ingredients for 10 minutes then cool thoroughly
> When cool add 2 t baking powder and 1 t baking soda. Then add enough flour to make a stiff batter. Add 1 C walnut pieces and bake in greased and floured loaf tins at 300 degrees for 1 hour or until firm.
