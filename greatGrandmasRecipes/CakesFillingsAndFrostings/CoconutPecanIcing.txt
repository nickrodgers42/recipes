# Coconut Pecan Icing
a:

> Combine 1 C evaporated milk, 1 C sugar, 4 egg yolks or two eggs, 1/2 C butter, 1 t vanilla.
> Cook over medium heat until mixture thickens, about 12 minutes. Add 1 1/3 C flaked coconut, 1 C chopped pecans or walnuts. Beat until frosting is cool and thick.
> Makes 2 2/3 C.
