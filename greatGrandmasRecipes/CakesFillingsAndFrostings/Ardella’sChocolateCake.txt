# Ardella's Chocolate Cake
a:Ardella Baugh
* 2 C cake flour
* 1 t vanilla
* 1 1/4 t baking soda
* 1/3 C boiling water
* 1/4 t salt
* 1/2 C shortening
* 3/4 C sour milk, or buttermilk
* 1 C sugar
* 2 sq melted chocolate (1/4 C cocoa)
* 2 eggs

> Sift dry ingredients 3 times. Cream butter and sugar. Add eggs, beaten until thick. Blend in chocolate alternately with flour and milk. Last, add boiling water. Beat well and pour into a well greased 8 X 10 inch pan and bake 45 minutes at 350 degrees.
