# Orange Cake
a:
* 2 1/4 C cake flour
* 3 egg whites, beaten very stiff
* 1/2 t salt
* 3 egg yolks, well beaten
* 1/2 C butter
* 1/2 C orange juice, pulp, grated rind
* 1 C sugar
* 1/2 C evaporated, condensed milk

> Sift flour before measuring, sift again with dry ingredients. Cream butter and sugar, mix well with egg yolks. Add flour mixture in thirds, alternately with orange juice and water.
> Fold in stiffly beaten egg whites. For MARBLE CAKE add 1/8 t soda, 1 T milk and 3 squares melted chocolate to half of batter. Bake in two greased layer pans at 350 degrees for 25 to 30 minutes
