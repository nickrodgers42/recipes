# Rice Crispie Balls
a:Leatha Lundahl
* 3 C Rice Crispies
* 12 large marshmallows
* 1 C sugar, caramelize
* 1/2 C condensed milk
* 1 C coconut 1 t vanilla
* 1 C chopped nuts

> Add milk and vanilla to caramelize sugar and stir until all lumps are melted. Boil slightly and pour over remaining ingredients. Form into balls.
