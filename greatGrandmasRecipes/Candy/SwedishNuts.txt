# Swedish Nuts
a:
* 1 C whole almonds
* 1 C walnut halves
* 1 C pecan halves
* (Can be any combination of 3 cups nuts)

> Beat 1 C sugar and a dash of salt into 2 stiffly beaten egg whites. Fold in nuts. Melt 1/2 C butter in 15 x 10 inch pan. Spread mixture over butter. Bake at 325 degrees 30 minutes, stirring until nuts are coated and a little brown and butter is absorbed.
