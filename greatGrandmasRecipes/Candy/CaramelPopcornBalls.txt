# Caramel Popcorn Balls
a:Cherrill Nielsen
* 2 C brown sugar
* 1/4 lb butter or margarine
* 1/2 C white corn syrup
* 3/4 C water

> Boil to soft ball stage and then add 12 large marshmallows. Beat and pour over popped corn. Form into balls. 1 2/3 C unpopped corn makes about the right amount of popped corn.
