# Angel Sweets
a:
* 1 lb chocolate chips
* 2 T butter
* 1 egg
* 1 C walnuts
* 2 C small marshmallows (colored ones are pretty)
* 1/2 C flaked coconut

> Melt chocolate and butter over low heat. Remove from heat and blend in beaten egg. Stir in remaining ingredients and mix well. Shape into 1 inch balls or form into log. Roll in coconut. Wrap log in wax paper. Makes 48 balls.
