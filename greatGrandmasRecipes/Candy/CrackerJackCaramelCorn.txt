# Cracker Jack Caramel Corn
a:Sylvester Anderson
* 6 to 7 quarts popped corn
* 2 T molasses
* 1 lb brown sugar
* 1/4 lb butter or margarine

> Book to soft ball stage and add 1 t vinegar and 1 t soda. Stir to foam. Pour over popped corn. Mix well and then put on 2 cookie sheets and bake at 200 degrees for 5 to 15 minutes. Watch carefully and stir a few times. As it cools separate kernels, or leave in small clusters. Cool thoroughly before storing in air-tight containers.
