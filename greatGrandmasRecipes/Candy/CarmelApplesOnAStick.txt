# Carmel Apples On A Stick
a:

> Heat 1 lb (56) vanilla caramels and 2 T water over low heat until melted. Stir until smooth.
> Put sticks or wooden skewers into blossom ends of 6 crisp, medium apples. Dip each apple into caramel syrup and turn until surface is completely coated. (If syrup is too stiff add a few drops of water) At once roll bottom half each apple in 1/2 C chopped walnuts (approximate). Set on wax paper covered cookie sheet and refrigerate until coating is firm.
