# Fondant
a:USU Extension Service
* 4 C sugar
* 1 C water
* 1/2 C glucose or 2/3 C corn syrup

> Combine ingredients and stir until dissolved. Bring to a boil and wash sides of kettle down by placing cover on kettle for a few seconds. Cook to 232 degrees and pour on cold slab or plate. Cool slightly and beat slowly until grain forms and it gets hard. Place in covered jar or crock to age for a few days. Without a thermometer, cook fondant to a firm ball stage. When ready to use. knead fondant with hands until soft and dough-like. Use as cream centers, or wrap drained cherries to be clipped in dipping chocolate. or melt over hot water to dip caramels for, Merry Widows.
