# Ice Cream Taffy
a:
* 3 C sugar
* 1/4 C white corn syrup
* 2 T vinegar
* 1 C boiling water
* 2 T butter
* 1 t vanilla or other flavoring

> Mix sugar, water, syrup and vinegar. Wipe down sides of kettle. Cover and bring slowly to a boil. Remove cover and cook to crack stage, 290 degrees. Cool on buttered platter or marble slab. Flavor and pull until satiny. Pull into ropes and cut into desired sized pieces.
> Nuts may be added if desired before pulling.
