# White Fudge
a:Elizabeth Thorpe
* 4 C sugar
* 1/2 cube butter
* 1 C cream or 1 C canned milk plus 1/4 C water
* 2 T white corn syrup
* 1 C nuts
* 1/2 t salt
* 1 t vanilla

> Cook sugar, syrup, butter and cream to 224 degrees or soft ball stage. Wash down sides of kettle at first until all crystals are gone. Stir all the while. Pour on greased slab or platter to cool. Then beat until stiff enough to set. Add vanilla and nuts. Pour into greased mold.
> Cut when cold. Takes about 10 minutes to cook and 20 minutes to beat.
