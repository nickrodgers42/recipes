# Date Roll
a:Leda Peterson
* 2 C sugar
* 1 T butter
* 1 1/2 C pitted dates cut into thirds
* 1 T vanilla
* 1 C milk
* 1 C chopped dates

> Cook sugar and milk to soft ball stage. Add dates and cook until they seem to melt. (About 5 minutes) Remove from heat, add butter. vanilla and nuts. Cool slightly. Beat while warm until creamy. Pour into wet cloth and roll up into a sausage shape, about 1 1/2 inch in diameter. Tie each end and hang until cold. Cut into slices.
