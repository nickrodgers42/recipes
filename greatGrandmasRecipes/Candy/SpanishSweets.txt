# Spanish Sweets
a:

> Put through food chopper; 2 C seeded dates, 1 C dried figs, 3/4 C raisins. Add 1/3 C candied cherries cut up, 1/3 C candied pineapple shredded, 1/2 C chopped pecans or walnuts, 2 T (or more) orange juice, and 1/8 t salt. Work into a solid mass and then press into a wax paper lined pan. Refrigerate for at least 24 hours before slicing.
> This is an oldie that I used when I was first married. The writing in my old cookbook is very dim now. We liked this recipe.
