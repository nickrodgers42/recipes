# Shrimp Salad
a:Ruth Baugh
* 2 pkg lemon Jell-o
* 2 C boiling water
* 8 oz cream cheese
* 1/2 C light cream
* 1 C chopped olives
* 1 C celery, finely chopped
* 1 C whipping cream
* 1/2 C mayonnaise
* 1/2 C Miracle Whip
* 2 t lemon juice
* 1 1/2 t chopped green onion
* 4 T chopped pimento
* 1 1/2 C (2 cans) broken shrimp

Chill until firm. Mix all other ingredients and allow to marinate in refrigerator for several hours before topping salad mixture. Serve cold.
