# Tomato Seafood Salad
a:
* 1 6 oz pkg lemon Jell-o
* 1 t grated onion
* 1 t vinegar
* 2 C tomato juice
* 1 C finely chopped celery
* 1/2 t salt
* 2 small cans tomato sauce
* 1 can shrimp, crab or tuna
* 1 t sugar
* 1/2 t monosodium glutamate

> Boil tomato juice and sauce and stir into Jell-o. Cool until partially set and add remaining ingredients. Pour into individual molds or one large mold. Serve on salad greens with a dressing of your choice.
