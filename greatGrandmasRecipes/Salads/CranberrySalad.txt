# Cranberry Salad
a:Lucy Ellis
* 1 3 oz pkg cherry Jell-o
* 1 C sugar
* 1 C pineapple juice
* 1 ground orange
* 1 C crushed pineapple
* 1 C chopped celery
* 1 C hot water
* 1 T lemon juice
* 1 C raw cranberries, ground up

> Dissolve Jell-o and sugar in hot water; add lemon and pineapple juice. Cool until syrupy.
> Add remaining ingredients, mixing well. Pour into mold and set until firm. Serve with mayonnaise or mayonnaise mixed with whipped cream.
