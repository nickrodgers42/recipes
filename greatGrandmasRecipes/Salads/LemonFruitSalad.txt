# Lemon Fruit Salad
a:Cherrill Nielsen
* 4 1/2 oz pkg lemon Pudding (not instant)
* l 13 1/2 oz tub whipped topping
* 110 1/2 oz pkg small marshmallows
* 2 30 oz cans fruit cocktail drained

> Make the pudding according to the directions on the box. Cool in refrigeratOr about 3 hours. Using a large bowl, beat cooled lemon filling with electric heater until creamy.
> Fold in topping and mix until welt blended. Fold in marstimaltows, drained fruit and 4 sliced bananas, if desired. Yields 20 1/2 C servings.
