# Cabbage Slaw
a:
* 2 t sugar
* 1/2 t celery
* 1 T salt
* 1 C white vinegar
* 3/4 C Mazola oil
* 1 t dry mustard

> Shred a large head of cabbage. Put into a large bowl alternately with slices of Bermuda onion. (1 or 2 onions). Sprinkle top with 7/8 C sugar. Set aside.
> Mix ingredients together.
> Bring to a boil and pour over cabbage. Cover quickly. Refrigerate 4 hours. Stir several times before using. Will keep a long time refrigerated.
