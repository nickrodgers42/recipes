# Fruit Salad With Dressing
a:
* 1 pkg small marshmallows
* 15 oz can cmshed pineapple
* 2 C grapefruit, cut up
* 1 C grated sharp cheese
* 2 eggs
* 3 T vinegar
* 3 T sugar
* 1 C whipped cream, sweeetened

> Drain fruit and combine with cheese. Beat eggs, add vinegar and sugar; cook until thick.
> Cool, fold in whipped cream and fruit and cheese. Allow to stand in refrigerator at least 1 hour before serving. Serves 6 to 8. Good variation: add 1 mashed banana and A C chopped pecans.
