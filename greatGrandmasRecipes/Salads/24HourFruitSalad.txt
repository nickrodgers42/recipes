# 24 Hour Fruit Salad
a:
* 3 eggs, beaten
* 2 to 4 T vinegar
* 1/4 C sugar
* 2 C small marshmallows
* 2 T butter
* 1 C whipping cream
* 2 C mandarin oranges
* 2 C pineapple chunks
* 1 large can fruit cocktail
* maraschino cherries

> Mix eggs, sugar and vinegar in double boiler and cook until thick, stirring constantly. Add butter and chill. Add whipped cream and pour over well drained fruit and marshmallows.
> Mix lightly. Place cut cherries on top and refrigerate 24 hours. Do not freeze.
