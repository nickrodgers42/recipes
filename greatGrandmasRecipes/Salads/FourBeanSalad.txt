# Four Bean Salad
a:
* 1 can green beans, cut or long
* 1 can wax beans
* 1 can kidney beans
* 1 can garbanzo beans
* 1/2 C onion, chopped
* 1/2 C salad oil
* 1/2 C vinegar
* 1/2 C sugar
* 1 t salt
* 1 t pepper
* 1/2 C green pepper or 1 small jar pimento, chopped

> Mix all ingredients. Cover and chill 24 hours.
