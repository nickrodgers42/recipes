# Colorful Cabbage Slaw
a:
* 2 C shredded cabbage (red and/or green)
* 1 medium onion sliced very thin
* 3 T sugar
* 1 t salt
* 2 t salad oil
* 1/2 C chopped green pepper
* 3 T white vinegar
* Pimento if desired

> Toss vegetables together. In a small jar combine remaining ingredients and shake well.
> Pour over slaw and mix well. Garnish with pimento strips if desired. Serves 6.
