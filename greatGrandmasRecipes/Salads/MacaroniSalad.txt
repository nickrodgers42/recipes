# Macaroni Salad
a:
* 1/2 lb macaroni cooked in boiling salted water, drained and cooled.
* 1/2 C carrots, cooked
* 1/2 C chopped celery
* 8 oz peas, drained
* 2 T sliced green onions
* 1 can flaked tuna, drained (optional)
* 3/4 C mayonnaise
* 1 t dill weed
* 1 T vinegar or lemon juice
* 1 t salt
* 1/8 t pepper
* 2 t sugar
* 1/4 t paprika
* 1/2 t dry mustard

> Blend and pour over macaroni; garnish with hard boiled eggs.
