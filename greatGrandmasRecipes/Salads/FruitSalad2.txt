# Fruit Salad
a:Ellen Everton
* 1 3 oz pkg lemon or lime Jell-o
* 1 C boiling water
* 1 Can frozen limeade
* 1 C ice milk
* drained pineapple, cubed banana, and or fruit cocktail

> Dissolve Jell-o in hot water. Add limeade and ice milk to hot mixture and dissolve. Add drained pineapple, cubed banana or fruit cocktail. Refrigerate and allow to set. Cover with sauce given in the above fruit salad and chill again.
