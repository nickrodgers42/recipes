# Frozen Fruit Salad
a:
* 2 T light cream
* 1 3oz pkg cream cheese
* 1 1/2 C small marshmallows
* 1/3 C salad dressing or mayonnaise
* 1/2 C maraschino cherries, cut up
* 1 C crushed pineapple
* 1 C mandarin oranges
* 1 C cream, whipped
* 1/4 C sugar 1/3 t salt
* 1 T lemon juice

> Drain fruit. Mix all ingredients, add whipped cream last. Put in tray and freeze. Serves 8.
