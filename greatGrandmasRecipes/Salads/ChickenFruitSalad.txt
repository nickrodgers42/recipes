# Chicken Fruit Salad
a:
* 3 C cooked chicken chunks
* 3/4 C red or green grapes
* 11 oz can mandarin oranges, drained
* 1/4 C salad dressing
* 3/4 C chopped celery
* 20 oz can drained pineapple chunks
* 1/4 C chopped pecans
* 1/8 t salt

> Gently mix all ingredients. Chill. Serve on lettuce leaves. Garnish as desired.
