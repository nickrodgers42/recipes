# Play Dough You Can Eat
a:

> Sprinkle 1 package of dry yeast into 1/2 C of very warm water. Stir until yeast is dissolved.
> Mix in: 1 egg, 1/4 C honey, 1/4 C shortening, 1 t salt.
> Stir in flour, a little at a time, until you have a ball of dough that is not too sticky (about 5 cups of flour).
> Knead dough 5 minutes. on waxed paper.
> Make figures on a cookie sheet. Make only flat figures as the dough will rise.
> Cover the figure with a towel and let rise in a warm place for about 25 minutes.
> Bake about 20 minutes at 350 degrees.
> If you don't want to eat the sculpture, you can shellac it when it cools and it will keep for a long time.
