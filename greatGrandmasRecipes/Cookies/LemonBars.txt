# Lemon Bars
a:Gayle Burtenshaw
* 1 C margarine or butter
* 1/2 C powdered sugar
* 2 C flour
* 4 eggs beaten
* 2 C sugar
* 1/2 C flour
* 4 to 6 T lemon juice
* 1 t baking powder

> Mix together margarine, sugar, and flour  and press into 9 x 13 pan. Bake for 15 minutes at 350 degrees.
> Mix dry ingredients then add to eggs and juice. Pour over slightly cooled crust. Bake at 350 degrees for 25 minutes. When cool sprinkle with powdered sugar and cut.
