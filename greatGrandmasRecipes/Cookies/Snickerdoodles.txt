# Snickerdoodles
a:
* 1 C shortening
* 1 1/2 C sugar
* 2 eggs
* 2 3/4 C sifted flour
* 2 t cream of tartar
* 1 t soda
* 1/2 t salt

> Cream shortening, add sugar and eggs; beat well. Add sifted dry ingredients.
> CHILL DOUGH. Roll into balls the size of small walnuts. Roll in a mixture of 2 T sugar and 2 t cinnamon. Place about 2 inches apart on ungreased sheet. Bake at 400 degrees for 8 to 10 minutes, until lightly browned, but still soft. These cookies puff up at first, then flatten out with crinkled tops. Makes 75.
