# Luscious Lemon Bars
a:
* 1 C flour
* 1/4 C powdered sugar
* 1/2 C butter
* 3 eggs (2/3 C)
* 1 C sugar
* 2 T flour
* 1/2 t baking powder
* 1/2 C lemon juice

> Blend flour, butter, and powdered sugar well and press evenly into a  9 inch square pan. Bake at 350 degrees for 15 minutes.
> Mix sifted dry ingredients with well beaten eggs and add lemon juice. Pour over crust and return to oven and bake 25 minutes. Cool on rack. Spread on icing when cold, and top with toasted sliced nuts. Cut into 2A x 1 inch bars.
> ICING: Blend 1/2 C sifted powdered sugar, 1 1/2 t cream and 1/4 t vanilla extract until smooth.
