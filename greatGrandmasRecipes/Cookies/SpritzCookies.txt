# Spritz Cookies
a:Martha Hug
* 1 C butter
* 1 egg beaten
* 2 1/2 C flour
* 1/2 t vanilla
* 3/4 C sugar
* 1/2 t lemon extract
* 1/4 t salt
* 1/2 t baking powder

> Have butter soft before creaming with sugar; add egg and flavorings. Add sifted dry ingredients in 3 additions, beating after each addition. Put through cookie press on to ungreased sheet. Bake 8 to 10 minutes at 400 degrees. Makes 7 dozen.
