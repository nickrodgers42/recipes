# Old Fashioned Sugar Cookies
a:
* 2 1/4 C sifted flour
* 1 1/2 1 baking powder
* 1/4 t salt
* 1/2 t nutmeg
* 1/2 T grated lemon rind
* 2 beaten eggs
* 1/2 C butter
* 1 C sugar
* 1 t cream

> Mix sifted dry ingredients with creamed butter, lemon rind and sugar, blending well. Beat in eggs and cream. Gradually blend in dry ingredients; beat well. Chill until firm enough to roll. Roll 1/8-inch thick on floured board. Cut 3/2 inch cookies; sprinkle with sugar.
> Bake on ungreased baking sheet at 400 degrees for 10 minutes. Makes 3 dozen. Dough can be made into a roll, wrapped in wax paper and refrigerated until well set, then sliced off in 1/8-inch slioes. Dip in sugar or a mixture of sugar and cinnamon and bake.
