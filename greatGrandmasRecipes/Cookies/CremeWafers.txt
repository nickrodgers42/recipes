# Creme Wafers
a:
* 1/4 C soft butter
* 3/4 C powdered sugar
* 1/2 egg yolk
* 1 t vanilla

> Thoroughly mix 1 C butter and 2 C flour; add 1/3 C whipping cream. Chill 1 hour. Roll dough 1/8 inch thick on floured board. Cut into 1/2 inch rounds. Coat both sides with granulated sugar. Place on cookie sheet and prick each oookie 4 times with a fork. Bake for 7 to 9 minutes at 375 degrees. Put 2 cooled cookies together.
> Mix listed ingredients all together and tint if desired. Makes about 5 dozen.
