# Macaroonies
a:
* 2 eggs
* 3/4 C sugar
* 1/2 C flour
* 1/2 t salt
* 1 t vanilla
* 1 T melted butter
* 1 6 oz pkg chocolate chips
* 1 t grated lemon rind
* 2 C flaked coconut, chopped

> Beat eggs until foamy, gradually add sugar; continue beating until thick, about 5 to 7 minutes. Fold in flour and butter and remaining ingredients. Mix well. Bake at 325 degrees for 10 to 12 minutes or until light brown. Cool 1 minute. Remove from sheet.
