# Cocoroons
a:
* 1 C sugar
* 2 C comflakes
* 2 egg whites, stiffly beaten
* 1/2 C flaked coconut
* 1/2 t salt
* 1/2 C chopped nuts
* 1/4 t almond extract

> Fold sugar and salt gradually into egg whites, carefully adding other ingredients. Drop from teaspoon on greased baking sheet. Bake at 350 degrees for 10 to 12 minutes.
> Makes 65. 1 t vanilla or other flavoring may be substituted for almond.
