# Meltaways
a:Cherrill Nielsen
* 1/2 lb soft butter
* 3/4 C cornstarch
* 1/3 C powdered sugar
* 1 C flour

> Mix; roll in 1-inch balls. Bake on ungreased sheet 12 minutes at 350 degrees. When cool, ice with 3 oz cream cheese, 1 t vanilla, 1 C powdered sugar. Makes 1 doz cookies. Make double batch. (Delicious but expensive.)
