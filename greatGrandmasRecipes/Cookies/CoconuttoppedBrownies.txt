# Coconut-topped Brownies
a:
* 4 oz Baker's Germanâ€˜s Sweet Chocolate
* 2/3 C sifted flour
* 2 beaten eggs
* 1/4 t salt
* 1/2 C sugar
* 1/2 t baking powder
* 1 t vanilla
* 1/3 C butter
* 1/2 C chopped nuts
* 1 1/3 C flaked coconut
* 1/4 C brown sugar, packed
* 2 T cream

> Melt chocolate and butter; Cool. Beat eggs, sugar and chocolate well. Add sifted dry ingredients; then nuts and vanilla. Spread in greased 8 inch pan.
> Mix cream, coconut and brown sugar and spread over dough. Bake at 350 degrees for 25 to 30 minutes. Makes 30.
