# Date Drop Cookies
a:
* 2 C chopped dates (FILLING)
* 3/4 C sugar (FILLING)
* 3/4 C water (FILLING)
* 1/2 C nuts. (FILLING)
* 1 C shortening
* 2 beaten eggs
* 12 C water 1 t soda
* 2 C brown sugar
* 1 t vanilla
* 3 1/2 C stirred flour
* 1/2 t salt
* 1/4 t cinnamon

> FILLING: Cook until thick, then cool.
> Cream shortening, sugar and eggs. Sift dry ingredients together and add alternately with water. Drop by spoons full onto un-greased baking sheet; put 1/2 t filling on top of each cookie, depressing dough a little. Cover with a spoonful of dough. Dough is quite soft.
> Bake at 375 degrees for 10 to 12 minutes. Makes about 3 dozen cookies.
