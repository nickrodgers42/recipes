# Cinnamon Jumbo Cookies
a:Bonnie Taylor Watkins
* 1/2 C sugar
* 3/2 C flour
* 1/2 t baking powder
* 2 eggs beaten
* 1/2 C butter
* 1 t soda
* 1 C sour cream
* 1/2 t salt
* 1 t vanilla

> Mix; Form in balls and roll in a mixture of 1/2 C sugar and 1 t cinnamon, or chopped nuts or coconut. Bake 375 degrees for 15 to 20 minutes.
