# Stir And Drop Sugar Cookies
a:Mary Alice Anderson
* 4 eggs beaten
* 1 1/3 C salad oil
* 2 t grated lemon rind
* 1 1/2 C sugar
* 4 C sifted flour
* 4 t baking powder
* 1 t salt
* 4 t vanilla

> Stir oil, vanilla, lemon rind and sugar into eggs; Blend until mixture thickens. Stir in sifted dry ingredients. Drop by teaspoons full onto greased baking sheet, shaping carefully.
> Dip oiled bottom of glass into sugar and press onto each cookie, putting sugar on top.
> Bake at 400 degrees about 8 minutes. They should not brown. Makes 95.
