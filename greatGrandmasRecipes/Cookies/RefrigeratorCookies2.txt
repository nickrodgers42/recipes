# Refrigerator Cookies
a:La Vera Coombs
* 1/2 C shortening
* 1/2 C brown sugar
* 3/4 C white sugar
* 1 beaten egg
* 1 t vanilla
* 1/2 C chopped nuts
* 2 C flour
* 1/2 t salt
* 2 t baking powder

> Sift flour with salt. Cream shortening and sugars, add eggs and vanilla, then dry ingredients and nuts. Mix well. Shape into a 1/2 inch roll; cover with waxpaper Chill.
> Slice thin. Bake on ungreased sheet at 400 degrees for about 10 minutes.
> Makes 68 cookies.
