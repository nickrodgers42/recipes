# Sugar Cookies
a:
* 1 t salt
* 1 1/2 C sugar
* 4 C flour
* 1 t soda
* 1 1/2 C shortening
* 1 t baking powder
* 1/2 C milk
* 2 beaten eggs
* 1 t vanilla
* 4 T sugar
* 2 t cinnamon.

> Mix together salt, sugar, flower, soda, shortening, baking powder.
> Beat together and add to dry mixture milk, eggs, vanilla.
> Flour hands, roll mixture into balls, and roll balls in mixture of sugar and cinnamon
> Bake at 375 degrees for 10 minutes. Instead of using sugar mixture, cookies may be frosted.
