# Boston Cookies
a:
* 1 C shortening
* 1 1/2 C sugar
* 3 eggs beaten
* 3 1/4 C flour
* 1 t soda
* 1/2 t salt
* 1/2 t hot water
* 1 t cinnamon
* 1/2 C currants
* 1/2 C chopped or ground raisins
* 1 C chopped nuts

> Dissolve soda in hot water and add to creamed butter, sugar and eggs. Add 1/2 of flour sifted with salt and cinnamon, then add fruit and remaining flour. Drop by spoons full 1-inch apart on greased cookie sheet. Bake 375 degrees 12 to 15 minutes. Makes about 108 cookies.
