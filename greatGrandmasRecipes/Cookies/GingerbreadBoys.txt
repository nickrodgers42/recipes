# Gingerbread Boys
a:
* 1 C molasses
* 1 T soda
* 1/2 t nutmeg
* 1 beaten egg
* 1 C brown sugar
* 4 1/2 to 5 C flour
* 1 t ginger
* 1/2 t salt
* 1 C shortening
* 1 t cinnamon

> Heat molasses, sugar, shortening and soda in heavy kettle until shortening melts and sugar dissolves. Cool thoroughly. Add egg then sifted dry ingredients. Mix well and chill.
> Roll out to A inch thickness; cut out and place on greased sheet. Bake at 375 degrees for 4 to 5 minutes. Decorate as desired. Makes 4 doz cookies.
