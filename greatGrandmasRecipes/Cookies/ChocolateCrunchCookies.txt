# Chocolate Crunch Cookies
a:
* 2 C sifted flour
* 1 C shortening
* 1 t vanilla
* 1/2 C chopped nuts
* 3 t baking powder
* 1 C brown sugar
* 1/2 C milk
* 1 C chocolate chips
* 1/2 t salt
* 1 egg, beaten
* 2 C crushed cornflakes

> Add sifted dry ingredients to creamed fat and sugar and remaining ingredients.
> Drop by spoons full on greased sheet. Bake at 375 degrees for about 12 minutes.
> Makes 5 dozen. 2 sq melted unsweetened chocolate may be substituted for chocolate chips.
