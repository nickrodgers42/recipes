# Toasted Coconut Drops
a:
* 1 C undiluted evaporated milk mixed with 1 T vinegar; set aside to sour.
* Brown 1 1/2 C coconut in 375 degree oven about 5 to 7 minutes, stirring often.
* 2 3/4 C flour
* 1 t soda
* 1/2 t baking powder
* 1/2 t salt
* 3/4 C butter
* 1/2 C brown sugar, packed
* 2 unbeaten eggs
* 1 t vanilla
* 1/4 t almond extract

> Sift together flower, baking powder, soda, and salt.
> Cream together butter, eggs, almond extract, brown sugar, vanilla.
> Beat well. Blend in alternately the soured milk and dry ingredients. Add 1/2 C un-blanched chopped almonds and toasted coconut. Chill 15 minutes. Drop by spoons full on greased baking sheet. Bake at 375 degrees for 10 to 12 minutes. Cool. Dip tops of cookies in butter frosting. Sprinkle with chopped almonds. about 1/2 C. Makes 5 dozen cookies.
> BUTTER FROSTING: Add 1/3 C melted butter to 2 C powdered sugar. Blend in 1/4 C boiling water and 1/4 t almond extract. Other nuts and flavors may be substituted.
