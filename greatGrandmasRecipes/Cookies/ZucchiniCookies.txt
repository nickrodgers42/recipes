# Zucchini Cookies
a:Cherrill Nielsen
* 1/2 C shortening
* 1/2 C sugar
* 1 C brown sugar
* 2 eggs beaten
* 1 t vanilla
* 1 t soda
* 1 t baking powder
* 1 t cinnamon
* 1/2 t salt
* 3 C flour
* 3 C grated zucchini

> Mix as for cookies and add more flour as needed. Add chocolate chips, nuts or raisins.
> Bake at 375 degrees for 8 to 10 minutes. Makes a real soft cookie.
