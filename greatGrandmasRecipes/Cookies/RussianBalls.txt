# Russian Balls
a:
* 1 C butter
* 1/2 C powdered sugar
* 2 1/2 C sifted flour
* 1/4 t salt
* 1 t vanilla

> Mix; mold into 1 inch balls and bake on ungreased sheet at 325 degrees for 15 to 20 minutes, or until lightly browned on top. Roll in sifted powdered sugar while hot. Makes 24 cookies. 1 C chopped pecans or other nuts may be added.
