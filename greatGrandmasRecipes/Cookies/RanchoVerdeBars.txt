# Rancho Verde Bars
a:
* 1 C water
* 1 C raisins
* 1 C sugar
* 1/3 C shortening
* 1/2 C grated carrot
* 1/2 t nutmeg
* 1 t cinnamon
* 1 t soda
* 2 C sifted flour
* 1/2 C nuts

> Cook raisins in water until soft. Cream sugar and shortening. Stir in grated carrot, then sifted dry ingredients along with raisins and water. Bake in greased 9 x 12 inch pan at 350 degrees for 15 minutes. Frost with powdered sugar icing.
