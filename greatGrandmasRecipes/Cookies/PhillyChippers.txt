# Philly Chippers
a:
* 1 C margarine
* 1 egg, beaten
* 1/2 t salt
* 8 oz pkg cream cheese
* 1 t vanilla
* 12 oz chocolate chips
* 3/4 C white sugar
* 2 1/2 C flour
* 1/2 C chopped nuts
* 3/4 C brown sugar, packed
* 1 t baking powder

> Combine margarine, cheese and sugar. Mix well. Blend in egg and vanilla. Add dry ingredients. Mix well. Add chocolate chips and nuts. Drop on greased baking sheet.
> Bake at 375 degrees for 12 to 15 minutes. Makes 5/2 dozen.
