# Sour Cream Cookies
a:
* 1 C sour cream
* 1 C shortening
* 3 beaten eggs
* 5 C flour
* 1 t salt
* 2 C sugar
* 1 t vanilla
* 1/2 t soda
* 3 t baking powder
* 1 1/2 C chopped nuts

> Mix together. Roll dough into balls like small walnuts, roll in 3 T sugar and 1 t cinnamon.
