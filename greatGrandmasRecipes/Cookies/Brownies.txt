# Brownies
a:Ida Harris
* 1/3 C butter
* 3/4 C sugar
* 1/4 C Karo syrup
* 2 beaten eggs
* 1 C flour
* 1/3 C oocoa
* 1/2 t salt
* nuts
* vanilla

> Add syrup and eggs to creamed butter and sugar. Blend in sifted dry ingredients. Add nuts and flavoring. Bake in 8 x 8 inch greased pan for about 18 minutes at 375 degrees.
