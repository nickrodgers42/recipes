# Butter Cookies
a:Olga Knapp
* 2 C sugar
* 1 lb butter
* 2 t vanilla
* 1 C shortening
* 4 eggs beaten
* 8 C flour
* 1/8 t soda

> Mix and put through a press. Bake on ungreased sheet at 350 degrees for 8 minutes.
> Recipe can be cut in half or fourths.
> In the mission field, my companion and I met Mrs. Olga Knapp who became a very dear friend. Although she nor her husband joined the church, they often invited us to their home for dinner. Mrs. Knapp was like a mother to us. She gave me this cookie recipe-- one of her specialties.
