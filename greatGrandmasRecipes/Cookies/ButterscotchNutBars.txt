# Butterscotch Nut Bars
a:
* 1/4 C butter (1/2 stick), melted
* 1 egg beaten
* 3/4 C sifted flour
* 1/4 t salt
* 1 C packed brown sugar
* 1/4 t vanilla
* 1 t baking powder
* 1/2 C chopped nuts

> Mix together; Bake in foil-lined, 8 inch square pan 20 to 25 minutes. Cut while warm;
> Cool, then lift block of cookies and over wrap with foil. Makes 24.
