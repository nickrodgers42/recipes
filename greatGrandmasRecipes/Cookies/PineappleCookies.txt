# Pineapple Cookies
a:Mary Hansen
* 1 C brown sugar
* 2 beaten eggs
* 1 C crushed pineapple, drained
* 2 t baking powder
* 1 C white sugar
* 1 t vanilla
* 1/2 t baking soda
* 1 C butter
* 4 C un-sifted flour
* 1/2 t salt
* 1 C chopped walnuts

> Cream butter and sugar, mix with eggs and vanilla. Add sifted dry ingredients. Dough will be very stiff. Chill. Drop by spoons full on oiled baking sheet. Bake at 350 degrees for 10 to 15 minutes. Frost if desired.
