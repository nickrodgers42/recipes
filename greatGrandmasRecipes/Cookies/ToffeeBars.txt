# Toffee Bars
a:Cherrill Nielsen
* 2 beaten eggs
* 1 C coconut
* 2 T flour
* 1 t vanilla
* 1 C brown sugar
* 1/2 t salt
* 1 t baking powder
* 1 C chopped nuts

> Combine 1 C brown sugar, 1/2 C soft butter or margarine, 1 C flour, pinch of salt;
> Press out in bottom of 9 x 13 inch pan. Bake 10 minutes at 350 degrees.
> Mix ingredients Spread over baked crust and bake for 25 minutes at 350 degrees, until golden and bubbly. Frost if desired. Cut into squares.
