# M&m Party Cookies
a:
* 1 C butter
* 1 C brown sugar
* 1/2 C white Sugar
* 2 eggs beaten
* 1 t vanilla
* 2 1/4 C sifted flour
* 1 t soda
* 1 t salt
* 1 1/2 C M&Ms
* 1 C nuts

> Add sifted dry ingredients to creamed fat, sugars, vanilla and eggs. Add nuts and 1/2 C M & Ms. Drop by spoons full on oiled baking sheet. Bake at 350 degrees for 12 minutes. Decorate tops with remaining M & Ms. (Nice for special occasions like Halloween or Christmas).
