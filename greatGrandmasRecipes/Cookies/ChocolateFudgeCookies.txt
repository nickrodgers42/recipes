# Chocolate Fudge Cookies
a:
* 2 squares un-sweetened chocolate
* 20 marshmallows
* 1 C milk
* 6 T sugar
* 3 1/2 C graham cracker crumbs
* 1 C walnuts, chopped
* 2 C sugar
* 1 t vanilla

> Boil milk and chocolate together for 1 minute; Add sugar and boil to soft ball stage (slightly softer than candy test;) be sure not to cook it too long. Remove from heat; add butter and marshmallows and beat until dissolved. Add nuts, crumbs and vanilla. Mix well. Drop by spoons full onto waxed paper. Do this as rapidly as possible, as it sets up quickly. Makes about 4 dozen cookies.
