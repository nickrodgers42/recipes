# Coconut Cookies
a:La Vera Coombs
* 1/2 C shortening
* 1/2 C white sugar
* 1/2 C brown sugar
* 1/2 t vanilla
* 1 C flour
* 1/2 t soda
* 1/2 t baking powder
* 1/2 t salt.

> Blend shortening, sugar, vanilla, then add sifted dry ingredients
> Add 1 egg unbeaten, then add 1 C coconut and 1 C quick cooking oatmeal. Mix well.
> Flour hands and roll into small balls, place on ungreased sheet and flatten a little. Bake at 350 degrees for about 8 minutes. Chopped nuts may be added. VARIATION: add 1/2 t orange rind, grated.
