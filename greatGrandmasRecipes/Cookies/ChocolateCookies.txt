# Chocolate Cookies
a:Cherrill Nielsen
* 4 1/2 C flour
* 1 C shortening
* 1 C sugar
* 1 C walnuts
* 1 t salt
* 2 eggs beaten
* 1 C canned milk
* 1 C Nestle's Instant chocolate drink mix

> Mix milk, chocolate and nuts together in small bowl. Cream butter, sugar and eggs; add chocolate mixture. Mix in salt and flour. (Will be a real stiff dough). Line 2 ice cube trays with foil. Press dough in pans and freeze. Slice off and bake at 350 degrees for about 10 minutes. Cool and ice with chocolate icing.
