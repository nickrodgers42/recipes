# Lemon Crisps
a:Cherrill Nielsen
* 1 C shortening
* 1/2 C brown sugar
* 1 T lemon juice
* 1 egg
* 2 1/2 C sifted flour
* 1 t salt
* 1 t lemon rind
* 1/4 t soda

> Cream shortening and sugars together. Add lemon juice, rind and egg; beat well. Add sifted dry ingredients. Mix well. Fill oookie press and form cookies on ungreased cookie sheet. Bake at 375 degrees for 10 to 12 minutes. Makes 7 dozen.
