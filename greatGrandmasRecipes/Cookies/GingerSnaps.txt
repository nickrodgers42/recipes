# Ginger Snaps
a:Elna Miller USU Extension Service
* 3/4 C shortening
* 1 C brown sugar
* 1/4 C light molasses
* 1 beaten egg
* 2 1/4 C sifted flour
* 1 t ginger
* 1 t cinnamon
* 1/4 t salt
* 1/2 t cloves
* 2 t soda

> Add molasses and egg to creamed shortening and sugar. Add sifted dry ingredients; mix well. Roll into small balls; place 2/2 inches apart on greased cookie sheet. Flatten slightly. Bake at 350 degrees 12 to 15 minutes. Makes 2 dozen large cookies.
