# Date Pinwheels
a:Mrs. Mary Windley
* 2 1/2 C dates chopped (COOK UNTIL THICK)
* 1 C sugar, white or brown (COOK UNTIL THICK)
* 1 C water (COOK UNTIL THICK)
* 1 C shortening
* 1 C white sugar
* 4 C flour
* 1 t soda
* 1 C brown sugar
* 3 beaten eggs
* 1 t salt
* 3 t orangejuice

> Cook until thick then add 1 C chopped nuts when cool.
> Mix eggs with creamed sugar and shortening. Add sifted dry ingredients. Divide dough and roll out. Spread on half the filling; roll up like jelly roll. Chill for at least 2 hours. Slice 1/4 inch slices and bake 12 to 15 minutes at 400 degrees.
