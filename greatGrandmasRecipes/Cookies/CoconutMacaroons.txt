# Coconut Macaroons
a:
* 1 1/4 C shortening
* 2 1/4 C sifted flour
* 1 C white sugar
* 1 C brown sugar, packed
* 2 t baking powder
* 1/3 t soda
* 1/2 t salt
* 2 eggs, beaten
* 2 C flaked coconut
* 1 C oatmeal (quick)
* 1 C chopped nuts

> Combine shortening and sugars; Add eggs. Mix in dry ingredients. Add oatmeal, nuts and coconut and mix this stiff batter with hands. Roll into small balls, place on un-greased sheet about 3 inches apart. Bake 12 to 15 minutes at 350 degrees.
> Remove from cookie sheet when slightly cool; place on wire rack. Makes 8 dozen.
