# Congo Bars
a:Cherrill Nielsen
* 2 1/4 C brown sugar
* 2/3 C oil or melted shortening
* 2 1/2 C sifted flour
* 3 eggs
* 1 t vanilla
* 2 1/2 t baking powder
* 1/2 t salt
* 6 oz chocolate chips
* 1 C chopped nuts

> Beat eggs and vanilla with creamed oil and sugar. Sift dry ingredients and add with chocolate chips and nuts. Spread this stiff dough out in a well greased 17 x 12 inch pan.
> You may have to hand press it. Bake at 350 degrees for 25 to 30 minutes. Cut while warm. May be dusted with powdered sugar after cutting into bars.
