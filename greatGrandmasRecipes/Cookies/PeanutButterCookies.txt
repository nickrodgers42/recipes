# Peanut Butter Cookies
a:Elizabeth Thorpe
* 1 1/2 C shortening
* 3/4 C peanut butter
* 2 t baking powder
* 2 t vanilla
* 1 C brown sugar
* 3 eggs beaten
* 1/2 t salt
* 3 C flour
* 1 C white sugar
* 1 t soda
* 1 C coconut

> Mix together then put on floured board and knead in more flour (about 1/2 C) so it isn't sticky. Roll into balls the size of a walnut. Put on greased sheet and press flat with fork.
> Bake 10 minutes at 400 degrees. Makes 4 doz cookies.
