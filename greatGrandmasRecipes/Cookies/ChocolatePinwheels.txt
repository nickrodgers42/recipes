# Chocolate Pinwheels
a:
* 1 1/2 C sifted flour
* 1/8 t salt
* 1/2 C butter
* 3 T milk
* 1/2 t baking powder
* 3/4 C sugar
* 1 egg yolk beaten
* 1 square melted chocolate
* 1 t vanilla

> Mix egg yolk with creamed butter and sugar. Add sifted dry ingredients. Mix well and divide into two parts. To one add the chocolate. Chill. Roll each half into rectangular sheet 1/8-inch thick. Place chocolate sheet on top. Roll as for jelly roll. Chill thoroughly.
> Cut into 1/8-inch slices. Bake on ungreased sheet at 400 degrees 5 minutes. Makes 3 1/2 dozen.
