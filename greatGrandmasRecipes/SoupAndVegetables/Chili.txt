# Chili
a:Ida Harris
* 4 C chili beans, washed
* 2 lbs coarsely ground beef
* Handful of chopped garlic
* 1 pint catsup
* 1 quart tomato juice
* 1 T salt
* 2 t chili powder
* 1/2 to 1 t cumin
* 1 chopped onion

> Cook beef, garlic and onion and add to beans when about done. Add other ingredients and cook until done. Chili has best flavor if made the day before it is to be used.
