# Green Beans With Bacon
a:
* 1 quart green beans
* 6 to 8 slices bacon, crumbled
* 1/2 C tomato juice
* 1/2 C grated cheese

> Heat beans and drain. Add bacon bits, and tomato juice. Simmer in frying pan after bacon grease is partly drained out. Add 1/2 C grated cheese; stir until melted. Serve immediately. Variation: Omit tomato juice, add 2 T dried onion soup mix and 2 T butter.
> Or A can chicken soup diluted 3 Iittie with bean juice and 2 T finely chopped onions.
