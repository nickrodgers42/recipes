# Orange Yams
a:
* 1 lb yams
* 1/4 C butter
* 1/2 C brown sugar
* 1/4 C walnuts
* juice and grated rind of 1 orange

> Cook yams in boiling salted water until almost tender. Peel and cut into desired pieces.
> Melt butter and blend with brown sugar, juice and rind. Place yams in buttered 2 quart casserole in layers. pouring juice mixture over layers. Sprinkle with nuts. Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes. Serves 8.
