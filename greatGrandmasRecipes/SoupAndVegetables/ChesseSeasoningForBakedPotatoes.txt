# Chesse Seasoning For Baked Potatoes
a:
* 1/2 C butter, softened
* 1 C shredded cheddar cheese (4 oz)
* 4 slices bacon, cooked and cmmbled
* 2 T sliced green onion
* 1 t Worcestershire sauce
* 6 medium sized potatoes, baked

> Combine all seasonings. Carefully remove potatoes from skins; mash potatoes until smooth. Add seasonings and mix well. Refill skins, topping each potato with buttered bread crumbs. Reheat for a few minutes.
