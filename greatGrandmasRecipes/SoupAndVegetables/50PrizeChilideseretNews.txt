# $50 Prize Chili (deseret News)
a:
* 3 C chili beans
* pinch of bay leaf
* 2 lbs ground beef
* 1/2 lb pork sausage (bulk)
* cayenne pepper to taste
* 3 sections of gartic
* 4 chopped onions
* 1 T chili powder
* 3/4 t cuny powder
* 1 T cumin
* 1 T salt
* 3 cans hot sauce or 1 quart tomatoes or juice

> Cooked washed beans with bay leaf until tender. Fry meat, onions and garlic until browned and add to the beans along with the remaining ingredients. Simmer 1 hour.
> Chili is better if used the second day.
