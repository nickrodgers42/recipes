# Creamed Onions Supreme
a:
* 2 C small onions, peeled and trimmed. (Sliced, larger onions are also very good).
* 2 T flour
* 2 T fat
* 1 tsalt
* 1 C milk
* pepper to taste

> Simmer onions in small amount of water until just tender. Drain.
> Blend flour and fat in heavy pan; add milk and stir while cooking until thick and smooth.
> Season to taste. Add cooked onions to sauce. If desired, creamed onions may be put into a greased casserole and topped with 1/2 C buttered soft bread crumbs, and browned in 370 degree oven for 15 to 20 minutes.
