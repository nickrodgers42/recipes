# Corn Custard
a:
* 1 can corn
* 2 T flour
* 2 T butter
* 2 beaten eggs
* 1 C milk
* 1 t salt

> Heat butter and flour together, add milk and salt; cook until thick; add corn and beaten eggs. Do not oook. Tum mixture into buttered baking dish and place dish in a pan of hot water. Bake at 350 degrees A to 3/4 hour or until firm. Serve hot. Makes 8 servings.
