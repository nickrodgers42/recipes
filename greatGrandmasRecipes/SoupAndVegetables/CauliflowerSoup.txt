# Cauliflower Soup
a:Cherrill Nielsen
* 1 head cauliflower
* 1/4 C butter
* 2/3 C chopped onion
* 2 T flour
* 2 C chicken broth
* 2 C light cream(half and Half)
* 1/2 t worcestershire sauce 3/4 t salt
* 1 C grated cheddar cheese chopped chives or parsley

> Cut cauliflower into florets and cook in boiling, salted water, drain and reserve liquid.
> Melt butter in a 3 quart saucepan; add chopped onion and cook until soft. Blend in flour.
> Add chicken broth and cook until mixture boils, stirring constantly. Add water to reserved liquid to make 1 C. Blend in light cream, Worcestershire sauce, salt and cauliflower.
> Bring to boil. Stir in grated cheddar cheese and sfir until melted. Serve sprinkled with chopped chives or parsley. Yield: 2 quarts.
