# Cauliflower Augratin
a:11th Ward Relief Society
* 1/3 bottle catsup
* 1/2 T Hickory Smoke (optional)
* 1 1/2 t mustard
* 1 head cauliflower
* 1 t salt
* 1 C evaporated milk or light cream
* 2 T butter
* 1/8 t paprika
* 3/4 C grated cheese
* 2 T flour

> Soak cauliflower in salt water A hour and then drain. Boil in salted water until tender.
> Heat butter, flour and seasonings; add milk and cook, stirring, until thick and smooth.
> Separate cauliflower into pieces and put into greased baking dish. Pour sauce over and sprinkle with cheese. Bake at 350 degrees about 20 minutes. Serves 6.
