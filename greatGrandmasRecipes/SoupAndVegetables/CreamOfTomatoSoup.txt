# Cream Of Tomato Soup
a:
* 1 pint tomatoes or juice
* 1/4 t soda
* 1 small onion, diced
* 1 t salt
* 2 t sugar
* pepper
* 1 quart milk
* 1/4 C flour
* 1/4 C butter

> Cook tomatoes, onion and sugar together for about 10 minutes; strain if desired. Add soda and seasonings. Add flour well blended with butter. Stir while cooking a few minutes. Scald milk in double boiler. Slowly add tomato mixture to milk. Serve at once in a hot dish.
