# Hash Browns Augratin
a:Gayle Burtenshaw
* 6 or 7 large potatoes
* 1 cube butter or margarine
* 1 1/2 to 2 C grated cheese
* 2 cans cream of chicken soup
* 1/2 to 1 pint sour cream
* 1/3 C grated onion
* 2 T melted butter
* 1 C crushed comflakes

> Boil potatoes, grate or cube into 9 x 13inch pan. Slowly cook onion in butter for 5 minutes. Sprinkle 1/2 C cheese over potatoes. Heat other ingredients. Pour over potatoes and mix lightly with fork. Mix melted butter with crushed comflakes. Sprinkle on top.
> Bake 1 hour or until bubbly at 375 degrees.
