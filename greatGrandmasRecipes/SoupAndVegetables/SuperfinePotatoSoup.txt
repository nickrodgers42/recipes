# Superfine Potato Soup
a:
* 3 medium sized potatoes, sliced
* 1 small onion, sliced
* 4 C hot water
* 1 C canned tomatoes
* salt and pepper
* 2 T butter
* 3 T meat drippings

> Cover and cook onions, potatoes and meat drippings with salt and pepper about 5 minutes. Add hot water; finish cooking. Add tomatoes and butter, boil up once and serve.
