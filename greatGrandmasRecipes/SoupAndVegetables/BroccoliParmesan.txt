# Broccoli Parmesan
a:
* 2 pkg frozen or 2 lbs fresh broccoli
* 2 T butter
* 1 C chopped onion
* 1 can chicken soup, undiluted
* 2/3 C milk
* 1/3 C grated Parmesan cheese

> Cook broccoli in boiling salted water till tender; drain well. Melt butter in saucepan. Add onion and cook till tender but not brown. Blend in soup, milk and cheese. Heat thoroughly. Serve hot over hot broccoli. Makes 6 servings.
