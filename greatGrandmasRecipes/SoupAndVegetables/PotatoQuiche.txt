# Potato Quiche
a:
* 4 medium potatoes, diced
* 1 large onion
* 4 slices bacon, crumbled
* 3 T bacon fat
* 1 C Half and Half, or milk
* pepper
* shredded cheese

> Cook potatoes and onion in salted water until done. There should be about 1 C water left on them after cooking. Add and heat together bacon bits, 3 T bacon fat and milk.
> Serve hot in soup dishes sprinkled with pepper and topped with generous amounts of shredded cheese.
