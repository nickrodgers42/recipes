# Candied Yams Or Sweet Potatoes
a:
* 6 medium boiled yams peeled and sliced.
* 1 C brown sugar, packed
* 1/4 C butter
* 1/4 C water
* 1/2 t salt
* 2 whole cloves(optional)

> Place yams in shallow baking dish; sprinkle with broken nuts and small marshmallows.
> Baste often as it bakes for A hour at 325 degrees.
