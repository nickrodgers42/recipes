# Creamy Onion Soup
a:
* 2 medium sweet Spanish onions
* 1/4 C butter or margarine
* 2 T flour
* 1 C water
* 1 beef bouillon cube
* 4 C milk
* 1 t salt
* 1/2 t Worcestershire sauce
* dash nutmeg grated cheese, croutons
* condiments; chopped parsley,

> Peel and thinly slice onions to make 5 cups. Saute onion slowly in butter until transparent. Blend in flour. Add water, bcuillon cube, milk and seasonings. Heat gently just to boiling. Serve in heated mugs. Top with parsley. cheese and croutons. Makes
> 4 to 6 servings.
