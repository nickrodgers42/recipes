# Broccoli Onion Deluxe
a:
* 2 10 oz pkg frozen cut broccoli or 2 lbs fresh cooked broccoli. cut up
* 2 C whole small onions (sliced onions in same amount are very good)
* 4 T butter or margarine
* 2 T flour
* 1 C milk
* 1/4 t salt
* 1/4 C grated Parmesan cheese
* 1 C soft bread crumbs
* 1 3 oz pkg cream cheese, cubed
* dash pepper
