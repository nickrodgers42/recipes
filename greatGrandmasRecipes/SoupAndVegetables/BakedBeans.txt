# Baked Beans
a:Mildred Child
* 2 2 1/2 cans pork and beans
* 1/4 C brown sugar
* 2 strips bacon
* 1 large onion
* 2 T mustard
* 1 C Heinz catsup

> Mix catsup, mustard and sugar with beans. Cut onions in quite large pieces and put into middle of beans. Place two strips bacon over beans and bake 20 minutes at 350 degrees, then mix bacon in the beans. Cover and bake 4 hours at 250 degrees. A bean pot is best, but any heavy pot will do.
