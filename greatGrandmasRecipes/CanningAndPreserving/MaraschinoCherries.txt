# Maraschino Cherries
a:Grandma Katherine S. Baugh
* 6 lbs Royal Ann cherries
* 4 1/2 lbs sugar
* 1 oz red coloring
* 1 oz almond extract
* juice of 1 lemon
* 3 C water

> Wash and stem cherries; place in brine made of 4 T salt, 2 t alum, 2 quarts warm water.
> Allow to soak for 24 hours and then drain brine. Wash thoroughly and remove pits. Add to them the syrup made of sugar, water and coloring, and bring to a boil. Let cool.
> Repeat this for 3 mornings, then add lemon juice and extract, and bottle while hot.
> Makes 6 pints.
