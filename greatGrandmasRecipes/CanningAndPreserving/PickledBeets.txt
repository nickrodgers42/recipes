# Pickled Beets
a:Mother Alice Baugh
* 3 C vinegar
* 2 C water
* 1 C sugar

> Skin cooked beets cut into desired size pieces. Put into warm jars. Boil ingredients and pour over bottled beets. Seal in hot water bath for 10 minutes. Store in cool, dry, dark place. Needs at least three weeks curing time.
