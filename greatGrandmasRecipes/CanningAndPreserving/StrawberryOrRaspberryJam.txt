# Strawberry Or Raspberry Jam
a:Leatha Lundahl
* 2 C mashed, cut or whole berries
* 2 C water
* 1 3 1/2 oz pkg pectin
* 6 C sugar
* 1 pkg strawberry or raspbeny punch powder like Kool Aid, etc.

> Combine fruit, water, punch and pectin and bring to a boil. Add sugar, and bring to a boil again stirring constantly. Continue boiling 4 minutes. Remove from heat and stir a few minutes to reduce foam and to keep fruit from floating. Pour into sterile glasses, cover with paraffin wax. Makes 7 to 8 glasses.
