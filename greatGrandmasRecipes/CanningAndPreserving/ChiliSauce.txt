# Chili Sauce
a:Mother Baugh
* 12 large ripe tomatoes
* 4 large onions
* 1 C vinegar
* 1 C sugar
* 3 green peppers
* 1 to 2 T salt
* a little cayenne
* 1 T cinnamon (cook in bag)

> Put vegetables through chopper, add other ingredients boil 4 hours, or until thick as you desire. Makes about 6 pints.
