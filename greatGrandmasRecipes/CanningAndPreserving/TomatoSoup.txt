# Tomato Soup
a:
* 10 whole cloves
* 7 bay leaves
* 14 quarts peeled, sliced tomatoes
* 14 finely cut celery stalks
* 7 medium onions out fine
* 10 sprigs parsley chopped
* 3/4 C sugar
* 1/4 C salt.

> Boil together all ingredients except sugar and salt for 30 minutes or until tender.
> Add sugar and salt then  thicken with 14 T flour mixed with water. Cook all together and seal while hot. Process at 15 lbs for 25 minutes. Makes 18 #2 cans or 10 quart jars. Add butter to soup when heating to use.
