# Chili For Canning
a:
* 40 lbs chili beans cleaned and washed
* 20 pints Heinz catsup
* 20 quarts tomato juice
* 20 T or 1 1/2 C chili powder
* 60 lbs coarsely ground beef
* 60 sections garlic chopped
* 80 chopped onions
* 1 1/4 C salt
* 10 T cumin

> Beans need to be cooked partially. Meat needs to be browned and fat drained off. Mix ingredients and cook together so it can be put in cans or bottles while hot. Prepare and pressure according to latest Extension Service Canning instructions. Makes about 144 #2 1/2 (3 1/2 C) cans.
