# Creamed Corn To Freeze
a:
* 4 quarts raw corn cut off cob
* 1/3 C sugar
* 1 1/2 C half and half
* 4 T salt

> Mix together. Put in shallow pan; place in 350 degree oven. Cook until thick, stirring
> often. Put in bags or containers, cool and put in freezer. Be sure to allow head space
> with no air.
