# Frozen Berry Jam
a:Dorris Baugh & Cherrill Nielsen

> Mix 3/2 C mashed berries with 6 C sugar. Boil 3/4 C cold water and 1 pkg pectin. Boil 1 minute. Pour in jam and sugar; mix, and let rest for 2 to 3 hours, stirring occasionally.
> Put in sterile jars and let rest on cabinet overnight (24 hours). Put in freezer.
