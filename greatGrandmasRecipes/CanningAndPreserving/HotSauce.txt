# Hot Sauce
a:
* 3 large red sweet peppers ground
* 20 C ground, ripe tomatoes
* 3 large onions, ground
* 1/4 to 1/2 C red peppers, ground
* 1/3 C salt 6 C sugar
* 1 pint white vinegar
* 3 t cinnamon
* 3 t cloves
* 3 t nutmeg
* 3 t ginger

> Tie spices in cheesecloth bag and cook in above mixture until thick, about 1 1/2 hours.
> Bottle and seal while hot. Makes 7 pints.
