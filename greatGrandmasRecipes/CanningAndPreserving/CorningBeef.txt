# Corning Beef
a:
* For 100 lbs meat:
* 6 gallons water
* 12 lbs salt
* 4 lbs sugar
* 6 oz salt peter

> The brisket and plate are the cuts most commonly used for this purpose, although other pieces may be used. The meat is cut into 4 to 5 lb pieces. Pack the meat and apply the brine as for pork. The meat will be ready to use in about 10 days. Meat may be left in brine until used, but if left in much longer it will need to be parboiled.
